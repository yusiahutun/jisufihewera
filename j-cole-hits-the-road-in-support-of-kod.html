J. Cole Hits The Road In Support of KOD

SANTA MONICA (CelebrityAccess) J. Cole has announced he will take off on a 34-date tour of North America, taking Young Thug with him.

The tour kicks off in Miami Aug. 9 and travels to markets like Atlanta, Chicago, New York and Los Angeles before wrapping in Moston Oct. 10.

The tour supports KOD, released via Dreamville through Roc Nation / Interscope April 20 and debuted at No. 1. It is J. Cole’s fifth consecutie No. 1 on the Billboard 200. J. Cole was the first artist to Jay-Z’s Roc Nation label.

Tickets for the KOD tour go on sale to the general public beginning Saturday, May 12 at 10 a.m. local time at www.dreamville.com and LiveNation.com.
