Teen Mom: Latest news, episodes, spoilers and updates

Teen Mom is a popular American reality TV series which was late renamed to Teen Mom OG. The MTV series was the first spin-off of the cult classic 16 and Pregnant, which followed the lives of teenagers who were expecting. It follows the lives of young adults who are pregnant or have given birth as they juggle the struggles of being a new parent.
