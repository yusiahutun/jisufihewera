Early life

George was born on November 1, 1996, in London, England, in the United States. His full name is George Henry Davidson. He grew up together with his sister. He loved computers and video games during his childhood. This led him to pursue a degree in Computer Science.

Career

George is well known for creating YouTube Minecraft videos. His first video was known as Minecraft Endermen Destruction (Timelapse). He uploaded it on October 25, 2019. Most of his videos are challenge videos, and they follow the title format "Minecraft, but..." or "Minecraft Hunter VS Speedrunner".

In most of his videos, he collaborates with fellow YouTubers to achieve the desired goal. Among the Youtubers George collaborates with are Sapnap, BadBoyHalo, and Dream. GeorgeNotFound also participates in Minecraft Championships. These are tournaments for YouTubers and streamers.

The championships consist of 10 teams of members, each competing in series of 8 minigames. For each game, a team wins; they get points. In addition, teams are allowed to vote for games they might be wrong at. In the end, the two teams with the most points are left for the last round, known as Dodgebolt that determines the winner.
