Biography

Beetlejuice was born in Jersey City, NJ on June 2, 1968. Born Lester Napoleon Green and better known by the stage name of “Beetlejuice, this frequent Howard Stern Show comedy performer participated in the Killers of Comedy Tour. He and actor Jerry O’Connell were childhood acquaintances: O’Connell’s mother taught special education classes to a young Beetlejuice. He was afflicted from birth with both dwarfism and microcephaly. When he was a child, his father (whose brother was a circus performer) tried to enlist him in the Ringling Brothers side show, but his mother refused to go along with the plan.
