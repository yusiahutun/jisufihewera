D. J. Cotrona Bio, Age, Height, Dating, and Net Worth

Donald Joseph Cotrona popular as D. J. Cotrona is an American actor. He is best known for his role in the film G.I. Joe: Retaliation (2013) and as Seth Gecko in the El Rey Network horror television series From Dusk till Dawn.

Being a famous face, Cotrona’s fans are curious to know his love life. All knew, his previous relationship with actresses Adrianna Palicki and Eiza Gonzalez. What’s new in his dating life?

Who is D. J. Cotrona? Bio

A native of New Haven, Connecticut, United States, Cotrona was born on 23rd May 1980. His birth sign is Gemini. Further, he holds an American nationality and belongs to mixed (Italian, Polish, German, Hungarian, Austrian, and English) ethnic background.

His mother, Sheree, is a teacher, while his father, Donald, works for a recycling company.

After high school, he attended Northeastern University in Boston studying to be a lawyer; however, after doing a summer internship at a law firm, he realized he did not like working with lawyers. In his second-year university, he switched to acting. During his spring break, he went to visit a friend in Los Angeles and never returned to college.

D. J. Cotrona Career

Cotrona made his acting career debut with a role Donovan Alvarez in the popular TV series Law & Order: Special Victims Unit. That same year, he appeared in Skin followed by Hollywood Division.

In 2005, he landed a role in the movie Venom as Sean. Also, he starred in the 2006 Slamdance Film Festival film Love is the Drug.

From 2014-16, he played Seth Gecko in the series, From Dusk till Dawn, being part of the main cast.
Most recently, he appeared in Shazam! Alongside Jack Dylan Grazer, Lotta Losten, and Ross Butler.
