Jennifer Lopez 'channels Ben Affleck's ex-wife' with off-brand geek look

Jennifer Lopez has shown she can stun in just about anything, as she debuts her latest ‘geeky’ style.

The music star has shown she’s the definition of someone who can do both. Just a few days ago, she had the internet on their knees as she showed off her figure in a cut-out gown in Las Vegas. Now she’s back in Beverly Hills, and the new look is a win with fans.

JLo aces the ‘chic geek’ look

Whilst out on a coffee run, the On The Floor star could be seen wearing a white blouse, unbuttoned at the top underneath a gray knit sweater.

Sticking to the ‘office theme’ she wore a pair of loose-fitting gray trousers to match. She opted for a comfy shoe, choosing white sneakers instead of a high heel.

To top off the chic geek fashion look, Jennifer added a pair of black framed glasses and tied her hair into a slick pony.

Although a fairly different look for the singer, fans rushed to X, formerly known as Twitter to compliment the outfit.

“Slayyy mama,” wrote one.

“Jlo with glasses is [heart eye emoi],” said another.

Another wrote: “Love Lopez but she’s been looking a lot like Garner lately. Both beautiful women,” referring to Ben Affleck’s ex-wife, Jennifer Garner.

The star’s Instagram proves she can do both

The Jenny From The Block star has been killing the Instagram game at the moment, treating fans with her killer looks.

In her most recent post, she sported a skin-tight beige dress, where she proved the color was anything but boring.

The star didn’t even caption this post, as the pictures spoke a thousand words. Once again fans rushed to the comment to share their compliments.

“you’re so hottttt,” penned one.

Another said: “The prettiest woman above 50 in the world.”
