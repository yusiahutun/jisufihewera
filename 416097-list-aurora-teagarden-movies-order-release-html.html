List of all Aurora Teagarden movies in order of release

Aurora Teagarden is one of those mysteries that many people love to watch because they are a safe thrill and an adventure. In the movies, the producers take you to exciting places, and you get to see the dark side of people. Many fans have watched Aurora Teagarden movies in order of release because they can relate to one of the characters, the films have lots of suspense, and there are so many questions left than answers.

Aurora Teagarden mysteries are a collection of movies that follow the life of Aurora Teagarden. She is a librarian and amateur sleuth who investigates and solves murders with the help of some of her friends. The films take their inspiration from a series of eleven crime novels of the same name. In 2014, Hallmark Movies & Mysteries started broadcasting adaptations of the novels as original film series.

How many Aurora Teagarden mystery movies are there?

While the Aurora Teagarden book series has 11 novels, the Aurora Teagarden Mystery has 17 movies. As of June 2021, Hallmark has released 16 mystery movies. The 17th episode of the film will be out later that Hallmark is yet to announce.

The title of the 17th episode will be Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: Honeymoon, Honey Murder. Note that you will not find Aurora Teagarden movies on Netflix.
