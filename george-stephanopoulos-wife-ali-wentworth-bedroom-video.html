George Stephanopoulos' wife Ali commands attention in surprising bedroom video

George Stepanopoulos' wife Ali Wentworth surprised us all on Tuesday morning with a makeup free video from her marital bed.

Ali appeared to be topless, underneath a fluffy sheepskin-style blanket, on top of crisp white sheets, as she talked to the camera. 

The star took to Instagram to speak to her followers, to share an exciting announcement. The caption read: "We are doing a menopause episode! Text me your questions here! “Why do I have hot flashes”? “Is there medicine to take”? “Why did I start menopause at age 46”? Anything you want to know! I have experts to answer! #goaskali @shondaland @iheartradio."

The comments section was instantly filled with questions from her loyal fans, hoping to get answers on the upcoming show.

Taking to Instagram earlier on in the month, the star revealed her podcast, Ask Ali, has been nominated for an award. 

Her husband George showed his support as he popped up behind her and said: "Way to go honey," and she quipped: "Thanks George."

Her caption explained more and read: "#GoAskAli is officially nominated for a Webby Award in the Podcasts - Interview/Talk Show category! Tap the link in my bio to vote now. Voting Closes April 20th! #Webbys @shondaland @iheartradio #shondaland."

In other career-related news, Ali and George have a new documentary, Pretty Baby, which examines the life of legendary actress and model Brooke Shields as she grew up in the public eye, with her first modeling contract when she was 11.

The film will see Brooke looking back over pivotal moments of her life and explore a society that sexualizes young women and features George and Ali as producers.

Fans have applauded the project, as one shared: "So looking forward to #PrettyBaby. It is so relevant to the present day pressure put on young females."
