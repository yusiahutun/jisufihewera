Mop Dog Halloween Costume - Dog Dressed as a Mop for Halloween

Don't worry about dressing up this year, because one adorable dog has already won Halloween.

Keki is a Hungarian puli, a breed known for its long, corded coats that closely resemble—you guessed it—a mop. So of course, Keki had a strong advantage when it came to dressing up as that particular household cleaning tool.

[facebook ]https://www.facebook.com/2PointFarm/photos/a.174214166082148.1073741829.172479739588924/629308217239405/?type=3&theater[/facebook]

Keki's owner, and presumably the genius behind this idea, is a veterinarian with All Creatures Animal Hospital. She debuted her dog's look at the MainStrasse Paw-Rade—a Halloween parade for pets—in Covington, Kentucky on Sunday.

Of course, the amazingly literal costume has since gone viral.

Good luck coming up with something this clever next year!
