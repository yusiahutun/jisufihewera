Kelly Stables Biography, Age, Height, Husband, Net Worth

Kelly Stables is a gorgeous and talented American actress and a model who appears on stage and screens for television and movies. She is an actress who is most popularly known for her works and her role as “Melissa” in “Two and a Half Men”, “Eden Konkler” in “The Exes” and the role of “Kelly” in “Superstore.”

Early Life

Kelly was born on January 26, 1978, to her parents in Metropolitan St. Louis, Missouri, United States. Currently, she lives in Los Angeles, California, United States. She belongs to a Christian family. She is someone who keeps her family life private and has not yet revealed the name of her parents or has never mentioned anything about her siblings. Kelly’s hobbies are acting and traveling. Her favorite colors are blue and black and her favorite actor is Johnny Depp. Kelly Stables completed her schooling at Lafayette High School in Wildwood. Kelly gained her educational bachelor’s degree in the field of communication with an Emphasis in TV and Theatre Arts. She completed her college studies at the University of Missouri, Columbia.

Kelly Stables Career

Kelly Stables has acted in many movies and plays. She played the lead role in her two largest movies which were Thousand Oaks Civic Light Opera’s “Peter Pan” where she played the role of Wendy and her role as the title character in “Sleeping Beauty.” She has even played other roles like that of Marion Davies in W.R. and the Daisy. She is best known for the stunts that she performed in “Rings” where she fought against the evil “Samara Morgan” in “The Ring Two” for which she is known all over the world. Her role in “Bring It On” boosted her popularity worldwide which helped her gain recognition in Rolling Stone, Star, and US Weekly.
