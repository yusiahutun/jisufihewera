'Cat like' Carol Vorderman has 'overdone the fillers', claims surgeon

The former Countdown hostess left fans in complete shock as she sported a swollen face on live TV.

Many rushed to Twitter to speculate she'd been nipping and tucking and now a cosmetic surgeon from MYA has addressed Carol's dramatic look.

The surgeon exclusively revealed: "It is possible that Carol may have overdone it with fillers.

"Fillers are an injectable non-surgical treatment designed to decrease the signs of ageing.

"Instead of affecting your facial muscles, fillers plump up skin sections as plumpness can be lost with the natural ageing process men and women go through."

They continued: "The procedure replaces soft tissue while filling in wrinkles/skin folds.

"We believe Carol has had more than recommended in her cheeks causing extreme plumpness and the face to look swollen or cat like."

The surgeon concluded: "We would also suggest Carol has anti-wrinkle injections to her forehead often referred to as Botox."

The 56-year-old telly icon hasn't publicly announced that she's ever been under the knife – but during a Loose Women appearance five years ago, she said she'd never rule it out.

Carol's been caught in a media whirlwind ever since and debates about her appearance have flown around left, right and centre.

Just three weeks ago Carol lost her 88-year-old mum Jean to cancer, having only announced back in March that her illness was terminal.

Jean was the driving force behind Carol's career and was the reason she was catapulted into the Countdown limelight.

Daily Star Online have reached out to Carol's representatives for comment.
