RHOC star Emily Simpson stuns with extreme slim down

Real Housewives of Orange County star Emily Simpson has documented her health and fitness journey for years on the hit Bravo show.

And now the 47-year-old appears to have reached a new milestone in her battle with the bulge. 

Emily looked slimmer than ever in new photos shared to Instagram on Friday, donning a skintight black bodysuit that hugged her slender figure.

The photos sent her followers into a frenzy, with many lavishing praise on the Ohio-born beauty.

However, some trolls also accused her of using the popular diabetic drug Ozempic, which has recently been used by celebrities to lose weight.

The slender star was accused of using Ozempic to drop weight, despite having been on a very public fitness journey for the last few years after gaining weight in 2019 (right)

'How’s that ozempic working out for you?' remarked one, while another sniped: 'Oh oh oh Ozempic, you know! You look fabulous but you looked fabulous pre-Ozempic too.'

Despite some nasty accusations, many of Emily's fans also showered her with positive comments and praised her hard work in the gym.

'Omg. You look amazing!!!! What’s your secret!' exclaimed one stunned admirer.

'I think you look great and I know you are loving those smaller sizes,' wrote another, before adding: 'Please also know I thought you were beautiful at your heaviest on the show too.'

While Emily didn't reveal the exact secret behind her new look, she did drop a hint by sharing a video of herself with her plastic surgeon.

The 47-year-old looked fit and fabulous as she posed next to her plastic surgeon Dr Siamak Agha this week

After losing weight, Emily also had a face and neck lift to accentuate her jawline

Posing alongside Dr Siamak Agha in his office, Emily said, 'I like to call this my second home because I'm here so often.'

She continued, 'We were talking about a lot of things that we've done in the past and some things we're going to do in the future.'

While she didn't reveal what she'd had done in the video, Dr Agha dropped a few hints by adding the hashtags 'body contouring', 'facelift', and 'breast fat transfer'.

Emily has been open about going under the knife before, admitting in February that she'd undergone a face and neck lift to get her jawline back.

'I still work out hard and work out consistently, but once you reach a certain age there are just things you cannot change naturally. I choose a combo approach,' she said at the time.

The reality star spends plenty of time in the gym and often posts videos of her workouts to social media.

Emily has been hitting the gym over the last few years in a bid to keep fit and healthy. Pictured in June

The glamorous attorney had a fuller figure in 2019 (left), but has slimmed down after working out and eating better

In 2020, she teamed up with personal trainer Paulina Hefferan to launch an online fitness program for women.

At the time, she'd lost a drastic amount of weight after embarking on a new diet and exercise regimen. 

'I'm ashamed of where I let myself get, but here I am showing my transformation because I want to be transparent with you all,' she wrote in November, 2020.

'I worked my butt off to get here and I'm so proud of how far I've come and continue to go! I lost myself and my confidence through gaining 30 lbs in one year in front of millions of people on a reality TV show,' she continued. 

'Here I am a year and a half later feeling sexy, confident in my own skin, and like the EMILY I am meant to be!'

The brunette bombshell showed off her figure in a plunging dress in 2019

Emily rocked a curvier figure in 2018 (pictured) when she first joined RHOC

Emily previously suffered from serious hip issues and finally underwent hip surgery in 2019, before kicking off her new fitness journey.

Speaking about the experience last year, the star told Us Weekly: 'I spent, like, two years working out with a personal trainer just to try to get in really good shape and get my weight down.'

She added, 'Middle age and the whole hip thing and not being able to work out and not move did not do me any favors at all.'

Simpson also insisted that she doesn't like to deprive herself when it comes to indulging herself.

'It’s about balance. I mean, I still like to drink champagne and eat cupcakes and go out to dinner, but I try to work out as much as possible to kind of … I don’t wanna be miserable,' she explained. 

'I mean, I wanna do all those things, so I just try to keep it, like, a healthy balance'.
