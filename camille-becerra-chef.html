Chef Camille Becerra Pioneered Healthy, Chic Food

Utterly unique, multi-faceted, and stronger than first impressions might have you believe, the correlation between woman and diamond is undeniable. To celebrate The Forevermark Tribute™ Collection, we're sharing the stories of strong, accomplished women who are more than the sum of all their parts.

When Camille Becerra assembled her first grain bowl (the now Instagram-famous, strategically organized bowl of vegetables, pastel-colored dressing, and, of course, grains), she had no idea it would become the It-dish of healthy restaurants around the world.

As a chef and advocate of nutritious, farm-fresh ingredients, Becerra has spent the majority of her career opening restaurants in New York City that are more than just places to eat. They're beautifully decorated environments beloved by the fashion set where you can enjoy healthy food that's as delicious as it is pretty.

What makes each of Becerra's meals so exquisite—and photo friendly—is her ability to layer classic ingredients in visually stunning, unexpected ways. Much like mixing and matching the jewelry from the Forevermark Tribute™ Collection, every aspect of the dishes she prepares is thoughtfully considered.
