USM academic honors for spring semester | Education

The University of Southern Mississippi has released its President’s and Dean’s Lists for the 2023 spring semester. The President’s List includes full-time students who earned a 4.0 grade-point average (all A’s). Dean’s List scholars are those with at least a 3.5 GPA, but less than a 4.0. Local students who earned the honors are listed by town.
