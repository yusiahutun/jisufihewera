The Style of Shorts to Wear Based on Your Zodiac Sign

"When in doubt, consult the stars," is helpful advice whether you're making a big decision (like, say, between two potential love interests) or a small one (deciding on a new style of shorts to try). Whether or not you know your entire birth chart by heart, letting your zodiac sign be your shopping guide offers a few unexpected benefits. For one, it alleviates many familiar fashion pain points, like fear of a certain color because someone in middle school told you it looked dumb or avoiding daring silhouettes because they're out of your comfort zone. And that's certainly true of a summer staple, shorts.

That's not to say that if the shorts style paired with your zodiac sign below doesn't tickle your fancy, you should waste cash money on a pair you don't love. We're just saying that it's possible the preferences of your zodiac sign could reveal something about yourself — and that just might be that you look great in striped paperbag shorts. Keep scrolling to find out if that's the case.

Aquarius: Neon Lace Shorts

Aquarians take their individuality very seriously. As such, a pair of lace shorts in a vibrant shade of neon that stands out from the crowd will have them feeling their very best.

Pisces: Floral Shorts

This water sign is drawn to all things dreamy, so a pair of soft shorts with lots of movement and a swoon-worthy floral print will speak volumes to them.

Aries: a Sporty Skort

Often active and sporty if not downright competitive when it comes to their favorite physical activities, an Aries will feel right at home in a flouncy skort.

Taurus: Pink Shorts

When it comes to dressing like a Taurus, you can't go wrong with pink. It's the signature color of Venus, the planetary ruler of Taurus. While they don't follow trends, Taureans do favor comfort, making a pink short in a classic silhouette a sure thing for this sign.

Gemini : Sequin Shorts

Geminis love an eye-catching embellishment, and are drawn to bright colors and sequins.

Cancer: Cargo Shorts

Pockets are an important part of any 'fit for Cancers who rarely leave the house without stocking up on anything and everything they (and their friends) might need. As such, a pair of comfy cargo shorts will keep this sign feeling secure.

Leo: Statement Leather Shorts

Leos love a statement piece and shorts are no exception. While a number standout styles — including printed patterns and structured looks — fit this bill, nothing makes a statement quite like a pair of leather or leather-esque shorts.

Virgo: Tailored Shorts

Drawn to structure in life as well as in fashion, Virgos will feel sophisticated and confident in a pair of well-tailored shorts.

Libra: Pleated Shorts

Libras love color, balance, and glamour, but don't feel the need to go out of the way with an over-the-top fashion statement. As such, a pair of pleated shorts in a vibrant color are a great choice.

Scorpio: Satin Shorts

Typically identified as the sultry troublemakers of the zodiac, Scorpios actually gravitate towards timeless and classic fashion with just a dash of edge thrown in. Embrace the magnetic sexiness of this sign while honoring its classic sensibilities by adding satin shorts to your summer wardrobe.

Sagittarius: Colorful Denim Shorts

Always on the lookout for their next adventure, Sagittarians also love a look that relays their artistic sensibilities. Denim shorts in an unexpected color or, even better, an ombré-style blend of colors, will definitely get that job done.

Capricorn: Paperbag Shorts

The "CEO of the zodiac" Capricorns love a chic and slightly conservation vibe when it comes to fashion and accessories. A pair of linen paperbag shorts that exude quiet luxury is the perfect summertime staple for this sign.
