Sidemen charity match star Mr Beast has net worth four-times higher than KSI

The Sidemen’s clash against the YouTube AllStars may be a charity match, but there will not be too much friendliness.

While the YouTubers and influencers will be up against each other at the London Stadium, they have all been rivalling each other for space in the overpopulated and competitive social media universe.

The likes of KSI, Yung Filly, and iShowSpeed have all made huge amounts of money from their YouTube and social media accounts, but one member of the AllStars stands out from the rest.

That is Jimmy Donaldson, who you will likely know better as MrBeast.
