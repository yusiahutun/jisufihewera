Guess Which A-List Celebrity Kids Attend Sierra Canyon

A major question that puzzles us mere mortals is whether celebrity kids go to the same schools.

Given the huge public interest in them and their parents, there must be a special school for these ‘’nepotism babies’’ to protect them from the public glare, right?

Well, there are actually exclusive schools where celebrities prefer to send their children.

Of course, anyone with deep pockets can take their kids there, but these institutions are mostly saturated with celeb babies.

Sierra Canyon is one of these schools where kids born to the who’s who in society casually interact as they pursue their mostly arts- and sports-focused education.

So, which celebrity kids go to Sierra Canyon?

Truth be told, it’s not easy knowing due to privacy concerns, but we did a bit of digging and discovered quite a number of celeb babies who are current or former students at the prestigious institution.

Read on for all the deets!

Why is Sierra Canyon So Expensive?

Founded in 1972, Sierra Canyon began as a day camp, later evolving into a K-12 and incorporating a high school to become a non-sectarian, co-ed college preparatory.

Among other things, the school is known for its exorbitant fees.

According to the Sierra Canyon School website, tuition alone ranges from $20,000 to $35,000 a year for kids in lower school. Students in middle and upper school part with up to $43,000.

Sierra Canyon claims to prepare each student for a successful college or university pathway leading to a high-flying career.

The school boasts a 100% graduation rate, with many of its students pursuing successful sports and creative careers.

Famous Sierra Canyon Alumni

In all honesty, celebrities are not too willing to reveal where their children go to school for obvious security and privacy issues. So, it can be a bit hard to know the celebrity kids currently attending Sierra Canyon School.

That said, you might be thrilled to learn about former celebrity kids who graduated from the prestigious Chatsworth, CA, school.

Here are some notable Sierra Canyon alumni:

Corrine Fox

Jamie Foxx’s daughter, Corrine Fox, whom he shares with his little-known ex-girlfriend, attended Sierra Canyon, where she was a cheerleader and was even featured on the 2011 cover of the American Cheerleader.

Foxx is also an alumnus of the University of Southern California, from which she graduated in 2016. Corrine studied public relations, was a Pi Beta Phi member, and continued her cheerleading career.

Foxx’s daughter also attended the American Academy of Dramatic Arts and Howard Fine. She has starred in some notable films, such as 47 Meters Down: Uncaged (2019) and Safety (2020), as well as television shows, such as Beat Shazam and Netflix’s Dad, Stop Embarrassing Me! where she is an executive producer.

Like many Sierra Canyon alums, Corrinne Foxx is doing really well for herself!

Heaven Hart

Heaven Hart is the daughter of celebrity comedian Kevin Hart and his ex, Torrei.

She is a 2022 Sierra Canyon graduate, but what’s more impressive is that she was the salutatorian at the May event held at the campus grounds. A salutatorian is the second-ranking position of her class, such an impressive feat!

On graduation day, her proud dad was so excited that Heaven would be headed to college.

“I am so proud of my little girl. I am also proud of Sanny, my niece, who was the first in our family to go to and graduate from college. She has been an amazing example for Heav, and now my little girl is heading in the same direction,” Hart gushed in an Instagram post.

Amari Bailey

Named Mr. Basketball California, a Gold Coast League MVP, and an All-Area Player of the Year, Amari Bailey has achieved numerous great feats.

The American professional NBA player for the Charlotte Hornets was born in New Orleans and raised in Chicago by his entrepreneurial single mother.

Bailey later moved to the Sierra Canyon School to prepare for a basketball career. As a freshman, he was instrumental in his team’s Open Division state title at the California Interscholastic Federation.

Ireland Baldwin

Ireland Baldwin is another notable Sierra Canyon alumni. The daughter of actor Alec Baldwin and famous actress and film producer Kim Basinger started her modeling career in 2013 after joining the prestigious IMG modeling agency.

Ireland debuted her career in the New York Post’s swimwear editorial and has, since then, been a well-known international model.

In addition to having a famous mother and father, she is also the niece of notable actors, including William, Daniel, and Stephen Baldwin.

In case you didn’t know, here is a fan fact: Ireland and Hailey Beiber are cousins!

The 27-year-old also has three younger half-sisters and four half-brothers from her father’s second marriage to Hilaria Baldwin.

Bronny James

Sierra Canyon is popular for former and current NBA players to take their children. Bronny James, LeBron James’ son, is one of those lucky kids who attends an expensive school.

Bronny is one of the most popular young adults in the world, with over 7 million followers on Instagram. He has been praised for his smarts and athleticism on the court, making him a much sought-after player.

The young James transferred from Crossroads School to Sierra Canyon for his high school freshman year.

Currently a freshman at the University of South California (USC), Bronny plays for the USC Trojans. As a senior in high school, Lebron and Savannah’s eldest child was named a McDonald’s All-American.

We’re not saying these are the only celebrity kids who go to Sierra Canyon School or previously did; there are definitely others who do but do not publicly announce it due to security and privacy concerns.

That said, we sure do hope that we gave you a glimpse into the academic side of the life of the children of some of the world’s most famous personalities.
