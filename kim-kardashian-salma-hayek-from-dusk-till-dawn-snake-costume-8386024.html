Kim Kardashian Enlisted the Help of a Snake for Her Salma Hayek-Inspired Costume

How many costumes is too many costumes for Kim Kardashian? The limit does not exist, especially after her Halloween festivities included a mommy-and-me costume with her daughter North, a Bratz-doll group costume with her sister Khloé, and now, an homage to Salma Hayek that came just one day after the official holiday and what many (many being Mariah Carey's lambs) consider the first day of the winter holiday season. In an Instagram Reel, Kim wore a teeny-tiny bikini that recalled Salma Hayek's character, Santanico Pandemonium, from Robert Rodriguez and Quentin Tarantino's 1996 film From Dusk Till Dawn. For one more layer of authenticity, Kardashian posed with a snake to really embody the "most sinister woman to ever dance on the face of this earth."

She finished her look with a headdress and captioned the post with a simple caption: "From Dusk Til Dawn."

It didn't take long for Hayek to drop by the comments, where she wrote, "Bravo @kimkardashian. I'm so honored. You've brought back some wild memories … and a little PTSD. Kim wore it best."

People notes that the PTSD in her comment may be referring to the snake.

"Quentin told me, ‘Oh, by the way, you’re dancing with a snake.’ I said, ‘I can’t do that, I can’t do that. It’s my greatest fear,’” Hayek said to Yahoo! Entertainment in 2017. "I had to overcome my greatest fear. I had to go into a trance to dance. And there was no choreography. It was improvised. Because you can’t choreograph a snake — we don’t know what she’s going to do!”
