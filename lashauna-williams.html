Lashauna Williams - InStyle

Title: Contributing Writer

Education: Fashion Institute of Technology

Location: Mount Vernon, New York

Expertise: Fashion, Beauty

  • Lashauna Williams is a writer, editor, copywriter, and content strategist who is based in New York.
  • She was a Contributing Editor and Assistant Marketing Editor for InStyle in 2017, where she was responsible for managing markets across digital and print, including plus, maternity, shapewear, hosiery, and shoes.
  • Lashauna hopes to use her knowledge to grow within this industry, and eventually begin her own fashion ventures.

Experience

While in college, Lashauna had internships at Rachel Roy and Stella McCartney before beginning an internship at InStyle. She soon became an editor with InStyle, working in the digital space. She next became a copywriter for Macy's and Shapermint. Lashauna has been the Copywriter for Amazon Fashion for the past two years.

Education

Lashauna earned her Associates Degree in Fashion Merchandising from LIM before receiving her Bachelor's degree in Direct and Interactive Marketing from the Fashion Institute of Technology.

About InStyle

InStyle is an award-winning destination for fashion, beauty, and pop-culture, with an all-are-welcome ethos that swings open the proverbial velvet ropes. Whether you’re looking for a wardrobe refresh, haircut inspiration, or a story that’ll make sparkling cocktail-party conversation, our expert-connected coverage is where to start.
