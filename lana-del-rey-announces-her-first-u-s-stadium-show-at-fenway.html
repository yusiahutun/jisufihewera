Lana Del Rey Announces Her First U.S. Stadium Show At Fenway

BOSTON, MA (CelebrityAccess) — Singer-songwriter Lana Del Rey announced plans to headline her first-ever U.S. stadium show when she performs a one-off concert at Fenway Park in Boston, MA, on June 20, 2024.

The performance follows her successful headlining billet at the Coachella festival, as well as a sold-out arena run that hit markets across the U.S. with the first leg of the tour wrapping at Charleston Coliseum in West Virginia on October 5th.

The Fenway show also follows Del Rey’s performances at the Foro Sol in Mexico City on August 15 and 16 in front of more than 65,000 attendees, according to concert promoter OCESA.

Del Rey is touring in support of her ninth studio album, Did You Know That There’s a Tunnel Under Ocean Blvd.

Tickets will be available starting with an artist presale on Wednesday, May 8th starting at 10AM.
