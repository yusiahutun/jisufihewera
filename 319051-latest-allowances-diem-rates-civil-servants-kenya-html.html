Latest allowances and per diem rates for civil servants in Kenya

What are the current per diem rates in Kenya for civil servants? The Salaries and Remuneration Committee (SRC) sets the salaries and allowances for various job groups. For civil servants, the amount is standard and cannot be changed unless SRC effects change.

Did you know that per diem is Latin for by the day? Per diem rates in Kenya are the daily allowances that employees get to cater for their expenses when travelling for work. The amount is given to cater to lodging, meals, and other incidental expenses.

Per diem rates in Kenya in 2022

In February 2022, the per diem rates in Kenya for civil servants were reviewed. The SRC effected the changes on 2nd February 2022. The per diem rates are divided into clusters.

Cluster 1 rates are given when civil servants travel for work-related reasons to the cities in Kenya. These are Nairobi, Kisumu, Mombasa, and Nakuru.

Other allowances

SRC salaries and allowances for civil servants are reviewed periodically. Besides per diem allowances, the SRC also issues hardship and house allowances for all civil servants. These allowances were last reviewed in December 2014.

Hardship allowances

Hardship allowances offer compensation for the cost of living in areas classified as hardship zones. The amount is given at a flat rate across all civil servants in the same job group.
