Mudita the forgotten concept of sympathetic joy

Jul 8, 2024 Mudita also known as sympathetic joy is one of four bhramaviharas or attitudes that are a part of the dharma as described by the Buddha. So what is exactly Mudita? Mudita means sympathetic joy. Simply put it means, seeing you happy makes me happy. Sounds easy, but it’s a very difficult quality to possess. For starters, a compassionate kind human being is someone who feels sad when he or she sees anyone else in pain or hurt.
