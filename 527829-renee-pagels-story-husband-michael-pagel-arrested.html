Renee Pagel's story: Was her husband, Michael Pagel, arrested?

Stories of women who have died at the hands of their lovers have populated the airwaves in recent years. Most of the victims have died at the hands of their ex-partners. Renee Pagel's story is a case in point. Her ex-husband, Michael Pagel, stabbed her to death in August 2006. The case went cold for 13 years before the investigation team nabbed the killer.

Renee Pagel came to the spotlight in 2006 when she was murdered by her ex-husband. The pair had gone through a highly contentious divorce in which Michael wanted to get full custody of their three children. But the judge ruled in favour of Renee. Renee was working as a nurse practitioner and health instructor at the time of her death.

Who is Renee Pagel?

Renee is the woman who was found murdered in her bedroom in Rockfield, Michigan, on August 5, 2006. She worked as a nurse practitioner at Forest View Hospital and a health instructor at KISD. Pagel was 41 years old at the time of death.

She was born in Grand Rapids to Forrest and Doris DeMaagd. Renee grew up alongside her sister, Michelle DeMaagd. Was Renee Pagel married? Renee was divorced at the time of death.

Renee and her husband, Michael Pagel, had lived together for over ten years before they parted ways. Their divorce was contentious because Michael wanted full custody of their children, but the judge ruled in favour of Renee. They were blessed with three kids, Sarah, Joel, and Johannah Pagel.

Renee Pagel's death

Renee was murdered on August 5, 2006. When the incident took place, Renee was bedridden as she had undergone surgery to donate one of her kidneys. Her hands were badly mutilated as she struggled to fight off her attacker. She was stabbed more than 50 times in her head, torso, and face.

The case went cold for several years because there was insufficient evidence to incriminate the potential suspects. After 13 years, there appeared to be some light at the end of the tunnel. Michael Pagel's brother came forward and confessed that he had vital information that would help the authorities to nab the perpetrator.

He confessed that his brother had shown him the knife he used to murder Renee and then threw it into the river. The investigation team went to the scene and recovered the knife. Her ex-husband, Michael Pagel, was arrested in February 2020.

He pleaded guilty to the murder but claimed that he had hired his brother, Beau, to commit the crime. There was no evidence linking Beau to the crime. Therefore, the jurists handed Michael a 25 to 50-year sentence for the murder of his wife.

Renee Pagel's episode on Dateline

Renee's murder is the subject of the May 21, 2023, episode of the hit NBC Dateline show Window of Opportunity. The episode featured investigators who tried to get the evidence they needed to put Mike behind bars.
