What Is Jamie Campbell Bower Relationship Status? Girlfriend Or Wife?

James (Metcalfe) Campbell Bower is a British actor, singer, and model, best known for his iconic roles as Anthony Hope in Sweeney Todd: The Demon Barber of Fleet Street, Caius in The Twilight Saga, the young Gellert Grindelwald in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows – Part 1 and Fantastic Beasts: The Crimes of Grindelwald, and Jace Wayland in The Mortal Instruments: City of Bones. (Of course) he is also the lead vocalist, guitarist, and founding member of the band, Counterfeit. But, at the end of 2020, Jamie had gone solo after the band split up.

Lately, Jamie starred as Peter Ballard in season four of the renowned Netflix series Stranger Things, and fans no wonder were wondering about his relationship status, if he has a girlfriend or if he is already married. All of this we shall explore in the rest of the writing.

Is Jamie Campbell Bower Gay? Does He Have A Partner?

Jamie Campbell Bower is reportedly not gay. Despite openly dating beautiful women from the industry, a report back in the time accused Jamie Campbell of being gay and even dubbed him as “a lad in a frock”. And they had made this gossip based on his tendency to cross-dress himself. And Jamie in his defense had then said, he has liked wearing girl’s clothes since he was 14.

“It’s the dressing up that I love most, and I love performing, but I don’t feel that’s my calling. I can wear a dress, but as a boy, my look is sort of non-gender-specific. I love being a boy and I don’t want to be a girl, but I do like clothes.”

Ever since several media outlets have presented Jamie as straight and not gay.

So, does he have a partner, of the opposite sex?

The actor has been on social media to mostly document his travels and offer a glimpse at his love for photography. And his personal life is something that he does not discuss here.

Jamie Campbell Bower Dating History

Over the course, Jamie Campbell Bower has been on the news linked with one too many famous females.

So, the first time, the media outlets wrote about his love story, was around 2007 and 2009. He had dated English actress Zoe Graham for 2 years before splitting in January 2009. In 2009, Jamie then started secretly seeing his Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows – Part 1 co-star, Bonnie Wright. Their romance was confirmed in February 2010 and a year later in April, they confirmed their engagement; 2012, saw them amicably call off their engagement.

Jamie then dated Lily Collins, his The Mortal Instruments: City of Bones co-star, from June 2012 until August 2013. They reconciled in April 2015 and broke up again before the year-end. Towards the end of 2017, they one more time gave a hint that they had reconciled. But, again, the relationship came to an end in 2018.

The year 2013 saw Jamie have a fling with fashion blogger, Zina Charkoplia, and the next year he was rumored to have briefly dated Olivia Hann. Between 2014 and 2015, he went out with model Matilda Lowther.

Jamie Campbell Bower Relationship Status: Girlfriend Or Wife?

Most lately, Jamie Campbell was in a relationship with Ruby Quilter, a tattoo artist. They were said to have started dating in 2018. This year in July, Jamie had posted on social media a picture of his hand together with Ruby’s hand. And everyone took it as him officially claiming Ruby as his girlfriend. Around this time, Jamie had also started liking Ruby’s Instagram photos. Moreover, the London-born actor also had just got a new tattoo which he had flaunted over Snapchat and tagged Jamie.

So, despite the duo themselves actually confirming that they are dating, almost everyone thought it to be true that they were in a relationship.

However, fast forward to now, and it was unclear if they continued to be in love with each other. So, Jamie’s relationship status at the time was unclear. Although he did seem to have never married before.

So, while two of his famous exes Lily Collins and Bonnie Wright are already happily settled with their respective husband, Jamie here has never had a wife before.

(As of 2022) Jamie’s dad David Bower worked for the Gibson Guitar Corporation and his mom Anne Elizabeth (née Roseberry) was a music manager.

As for siblings, Jamie has a younger brother named Samuel Bower.

Other people from his family are his maternal grandfather Eric Norman Roseberry, maternal grandmother Elspeth Mary Campbell, and late maternal great-great-great-grandfather Sir John Campbell (Lieutenant Governor of Saint Vincent and the Grenadines).
