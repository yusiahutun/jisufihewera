About

K.D. Aubert is a 45 year old American Actress. Born Karen Denise Aubert on 6th December, 1978 in Shreveport, Louisiana, USA, she is famous for her roles as one of the four original Fantanas in TV commercials for the soft drink Fanta, Donna in the 2002 stoner comedy film Friday After Next, Little Red Riding Hood in the 2004 independent fantasy short film DysEnchanted, Nikki Wood and the First Evil in the WB/UPN supernatural drama series Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Giselle in the 2004 comedy film Soul Plane, Eliza in Frankenfish, Cherise in the 2005 romantic crime comedy-drama film In the Mix, Julie the Waitress in the 2008 improv comedy film The Grand, April May in the 2008 comedy film Surfer, Dude, and Stacey in the 2012 drama film Turning Point in a career that spans 1999—present and 1999–present.
