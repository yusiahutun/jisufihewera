Fearne Cotton's metallic boots we are green with envy!

'Calm' author is giving us serious shoe

    Fearne Cotton appeared on the Lorraine show on Wednesday morning and totally dazzled viewers with her green metallic designer boots! The mother-of-two also took to Instagram to share an 'outfit of the day' post with her fans showing the former Radio One presenter sporting a smart printed blouse, black skinny jeans and of course, her eye-catching boots, which are by high end designer Vivienne Westwood and are priced at £325. The mother-of-two later shared a live update with her fans on the social media platform's 'Instastories', where she uploaded a glamorous 'shoefie' of her footwear in all their glory. Big Brother's Emma Willis commented on the snap: "Love these boots."

    Fabulous Fearne rocked the green metallic boots 

    The 36-year-old often shares her outfit choices on Instagram – and close-up of her shoes feature heavily! Her most recent 'shoefie' was an up-close shot of a pair of satin ballet pumps by Miu Miu, which are priced at £400. Fearne loves quirky labels just as much as she coverts designer threads and it's always exciting to see what the wife of Jesse Wood will rock next.

    Fearne's boots are by Vivienne Westwood and are priced at £325

    The mother of Rex, four, and Honey, one, recently gave fans an insight into her sleepwear attire – revealing her love for luxury pyjamas. Fearne wore a pair of silk PJ's by Ascendo, which retail at an eye-watering £330 for the set. The decadent two-piece are made in navy and feature white polka dots, and are almost too good to wear to bed!

    Fearne uploaded the perfect 'Shoefie' to her Instagram account

    Fearne appeared on Lorraine to discuss her latest book, 'Calm' - a new release for 2018. The book is a self-help offering which aims to encourage readers to try and find their inner peace, and cope with everyday struggles in a more relaxed manor. The panellist has written a number of books – including cookery books and clean eating titles.
