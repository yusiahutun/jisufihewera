Is Tim Curry Gay? - GistFest

Many stars who nobody suspected to be gay have come out to identify as just that. This has happened several times, leaving many with a desire never to be blindsided again. 

As a result, movie stars like Tim Curry have been subjected to gay allegations. For several reasons, Curry has had to live with the allegations for quite a long time. Not only has he never had a wife or kids, but his breakout role was as a homosexual.

Keep reading to find the answer to the question, “Is Tim Curry gay?” 

Who Is Tim Curry?

Famously recognized as the “True King of Genre,” Tim Curry is an English actor/singer who has made a name for himself in the entertainment world. With his talent and drive, the veteran actor has breathed life into dozens of roles with a career that cut across film, television, theater, audiobooks, and video games.

Curry has played many significant roles. However, the one he is most known for is Dr. Frank-N-Furter in the film “The Rocky Horror Picture Show” (1975). His portrayal of the character was so perfect that it became unforgettable to those who watched him perform. From that moment on, it became difficult to separate his identity from the Doctor’s. 

Some of Curry’s other notable projects include “Annie” (1982), “Darkness in Legend” (1985), “Wadsworth in Clue” (1985), “It” (The Miniseries) (1990), “Home Alone 2: Lost in New York” (1992), “The Three Musketeers” (1993), and “Muppet Treasure Island” (1996). 

His long and versatile career has earned him up to four awards and 13 nominations from prestigious award-giving bodies. Curry’s career is thriving. However, the success also exposed Curry, who relishes privacy, to the glare of the limelight. 

Despite the attention, he kept most of his private business away from the public’s prying eyes. This, among other reasons, solidified rumors that made people wonder: “Is Tim Curry gay?” 

Is Tim Curry Gay? Reasons Behind The Rumors 

Curry has always had a healthy passion for acting. This is often obvious in the way he portrays his characters. Indeed, this very passion he channeled toward playing Dr. Frank-N-Furter left him open to gay rumors. 

This is because Dr. Frank-N-Furter, the character that played a significant role in Curry’s success story, was an eccentric transvestite scientist. Curry played the character so well that many fans associated him with the role in “The Rocky Horror” franchise. Also, since the Doctor was gay, Curry was also thought to be gay, hence the rumors. 

Another possible reason behind the rumors is that Curry has never tied the knot. His fans seemed to take it as proof that the actor is a closet gay, and he never bothered to correct the assumption. The closest Curry has come to getting married was in his early adult years. However, the ship never sailed, and nobody but him knows why. 

Has Tim Curry Gay Ever Dated a Woman? 

Curry has always intentionally kept his personal life away from the public. When allegations of being gay are leveled against him, he does not respond directly, seemingly unbothered by what people think.

The same goes for his love life; the actor does not comment. As such, there is barely any information on past women or men he has been with. The only relationship Curry is confirmed to have had, was with his talent manager, Marcia Hurwitz. 

The details are sketchy, but some sources believe the pair got romantically involved in 2005 before breaking up a few years later. The two remained friends after the alleged affair, and their professional relationship was kept intact too. 

Aside from Hurwitz, Curry is also rumored to have briefly dated Marina Drujko, a model-turned-writer. The short-lived romance reportedly occurred sometime in 2008. However, it was not confirmed by either party.   

Curry remains single even today and maintains the ambiguity around his sexuality. Other stars like him who have been subjected to gay rumors include Tyler Perry, Zach Efron, and Vin Diesel.
