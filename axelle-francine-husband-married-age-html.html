Axelle Francine [Tony Parker's Wife] Bio, Age, Married Life

EntertainmentBy LRU Writer | On: February 23, 2020

Axelle Francine is the CEO and image consultant of Le Petit Med Spa. Her spa focuses on non-invasive European techiques for beauty. 

Married To Tony Parker 

Axelle has been married to Tony Parker since 1st August 2014. The pair, who started dating in 2011, are parents to two children. 

Axelle Francine with husband Tony Parker and kids in January 2020 (Photo: Le Petit Med Spa's Instagram) 

Their kids are sons: Josh Parker (born 30th April 2014) & Liam Parker (born 28th July 2016). 

Who Is Tony Parker?

Tony Parker is a former NBA star. He started his NBA journey as number 28 pick of the 2001 NBA Draft for San Antonio Spurs.

Tony remained with the team until his retirement in 2018. During his time with the Spurs, he won four NBA titles and became one of the core team members. He was also a six-time All-Star.

Before his NBA journey, he used to play amateur football. He turned pro after joining Paris Bucket Racing. 

Tony Parker Net Worth

Tony has a net worth of $75 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. 

Husband’s First Marriage 

Tony was married to Desperate Housewives star Eva Longoria. The pair first had a civil ceremony in the presence of Paris mayor Bertrand Delanoë and football star Thierry Henry as the witness on 6th July 2007. 

Eva's Current Husband:- Jose Baston Net Worth, Children, Family

The legal ceremony was followed by a religious ceremony a day later in Eglise Saint-Germain-l'Auxerrois in Paris. 

However, the marriage hit by Tony’s alleged cheating rumors with Erin Barry. Erin is the ex-wife of Brent Barry; Barry is Tony’s former Spurs teammate. Reportedly, Eva found out about the affair via texts on Tony's phone. 

After the incident, the couple went ahead with a divorce; it was finalized on 28th January 2011. 

Age 

Axelle was born in 1982 in Paris, France. 

Axelle Francine Education & Career

Axelle has a Master’s degree in Journalism with a scientific specialization from Paris. The entrepreneur started her career as a reporter for fashion and beauty. In 2010, she came to New York as the French correspondence. 

A year later, Axelle moved to San Antonio, Texas. There, she started working as a personal stylist.

In 2015, she started her Image Consulting company. While working on the company, she had the idea of Le Petit Med Spa. The spa’s services include health, beauty, style, and empowerment in one roof. 

Axelle is also the honorary member of The Luminous. The organization aims to work for the equality of women.
