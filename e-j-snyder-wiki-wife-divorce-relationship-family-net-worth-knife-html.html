Career and Progression:

E.J Snyder joined US Army at the age of nineteen and served for twenty-five years. The combat veteran was assigned for both Operation Iraqi Freedom in 2004 and Gulf War in 1991. While he was in Army, he worked as a Survival & Tracking Instructor, Ranger Instructor, and Drill Sergeant, receiving the name "Skullcrusher."

Moreover, after retirement, he served as Government Contractor teaching survival skills to the probable Green Berets and other cadets attending the US Army SERE School. He currently works as a motivational speaker and volunteer for many charitable causes. Similarly, he has designed a knife called ‘Skullcrusher’s Xtreme Blade’ for the Warrior survivalist to support them in the wild and battlefield.

He has previously featured on Discovery Channel's show, ‘Naked and Afraid’ three times, ‘History Channel's Patton 360’, ‘TNT's 72 Hours’, and various movies and television shows.
