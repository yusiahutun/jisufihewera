Jeff Glor Bio, Salary, Net Worth, Wife, Parents &amp; Height

American journalist, Jeff Glor is popularly known as the anchor and managing editor of the CBS Evening News. Glor joined CBS News in 2007 as a co-anchor and later as a newsreader on the Saturday Early Show. He also served as a newsreader for The Early Show. Regarding his personal life, Glor is married to his fitness guru girlfriend turned wife, Nicole Glor with whom he shares two children, a son, and a daughter.

Jeff Glor’s Bio: Age, Parents & Siblings

Jeff Glor was born on 12th July 1975, in Tonawanda, New York under the birth sign Cancer. Glor is American as per nationality and as far as his ethnicity is concerned, he is White. His parents raised him in New York.

Regarding his academics, Glor attended Kenmore East High School, a public high school. In 1997, he graduated magna cum laude with dual degrees in journalism and economics from Syracuse University. While studying in the college, he was awarded the Henry J. Wolff prize.

Jeff Glor’s Career

Glor started his career in reporting in 2000 at WSTM-TV Syracuse. After working there for years, he joined WSTM as a part-time producer. Syracuse New Times named him ”Best Male News ANchor” and also one of the 40 most promising professionals under the age of 40.

Moreover, he also served as a weekend evening news anchor and a weekday reporter from 2003 to 2007 for WHDH-TV in Boston. Since 2007, Glor is working for CBS News, firstly, as a co-anchor and later newsreader on the Saturday Early Show. Further, he also anchored Sunday edition of the CBS Evening News from 2009 to 2010.

On 25th October 2017, CBS announced that he would become the new permanent anchor for the CBS Evening News replacing Scott Pelley. On 4th December 2017, he became the new permanent weekday anchor of the CBS Evening News.

Jeff Glor’s Personal Life: Married, Wife & Children

Jeff Glor is a married man. Glor tied the knot to his girlfriend turned wife, Nicole Glor, after having an affair for months. His wife is a fitness instructor and a former cheerleader. Moreover, the couple met at Syracuse University and eventually started dating henceforth, they are together.
