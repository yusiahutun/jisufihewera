Emmys: Kieran Culkin uses big win to ask for something special from his wife

Kieran Culkin has started the year with success followed by more success. After his major wins at the Golden Globes and the Critics Choice Awards, the Succession actor snagged another trophy at Emmys 2024. This time, Kieran did not miss the opportunity to ask for something special from his dear wife Jazz Charton.

The Emmys 2024 was a big night for Succession. The HBO drama swept the Emmys winning the best drama series along with three acting trophies for Kieran Culkin, Sarah Snook, and Matthew Macfadyen. The TV series also won in the writing and directing categories.

Kieran Culkin gets cheeky on Emmy stage

Emmys 2024 became a significant event for Hollywood icon Kieran Culkin, who won his first-ever Emmy for his compelling performance in Succession after being nominated three times. The 41-year-old celebrity looked emotional on stage and thanked his mother Patricia Brentrup for giving him a great childhood.

The actor did not want to miss out on the opportunity to ask for something special from his wife as he said: “Thank you for sharing your life with me and giving me two amazing kids, Kinsey Sioux, and Wilder Wolf.

“I love you so many and so much. And Jazz, I want more. You said! You said maybe if I win! I love you so much.”

“It was very rude”

While the adrenaline of the big win took over Kieran on stage, he later reflected on putting his wife ‘on a blast’ calling it rude.

The actor explained: “A couple of months ago – I have been asking for a while – my wife said maybe if you win the Emmy and I didn’t bring it up for months.”

He added: “Then when I won the Globe I said, ‘Remember what you said,’ and she didn’t remember and I spent all week nervous, and instead of talking to her like a human, I blasted her on stage and it was very rude.”
