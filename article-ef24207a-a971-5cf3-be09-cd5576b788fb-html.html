City of Laurel sticking with C-Spire | Local News

With a light agenda to start the new year, the Laurel City Council was short-winded and short-handed.

Only four members were present — President George Carmichael, Tony Thaxton, Tony Wheat and Travares Comegys — as the council unanimously approved the handful of items that were on the agenda. The meeting lasted 10 minutes.
