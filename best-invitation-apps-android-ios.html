The 8 Best Invitation Maker Apps for Android and iOS

Amid organizing an event, one of the many dreaded items on your to-do list is sending out invites. This doesn't have to be a great struggle. You can send and manage invitations with a few taps straight from your smartphone.

If you're looking for some beautiful and creative designs for your next event's invites, check out these crafty mobile invitation apps for Android and iPhone for seamless event planning.

1. 1Invites

Close

If you want to create stunning card invites quickly, 1invites offers a unique, hassle-free way to make invites for your next event. To help get you started, there are dozens of templates to choose from, categorized by event types.

1Invites offers all the editing tools you can imagine for personalizing your invitation. You can change the font, add graphics, edit the background, and add links. The linking feature is especially useful for locations, contact details, or website links to your invitation.

Don't worry if you can't cram everything onto one page—1Invites lets you add multiple pages to your invite for a neater look.

Download: 1Invites for Android | iOS (Free, subscription available)

2. Invitd

Close

Handling multiple invites isn't easy. Invitd helps to reduce the stress and confusion of managing the process with its fast invitation maker. Simply add an event name and location, choose a design, then send invites to your contacts through a text message.

Guests can RSVP for an event with one tap by clicking a link on your invite. You can also limit additional guests per invitation and choose to be notified of new responses. If you struggle to keep track of numbers and dislike endless planning, Invitd will add ease and efficiency to organizing your next event.

Download: Invitd for Android | iOS (Free, subscription available)

3. Evite

Close

If you're looking to send creative invitations, Evite offers some fun designs for any event. You can access a wide range of event categories on the homepage, including birthdays, BBQs, and weddings. Evite offers both free and paid templates, with plenty of designs in each.

Editing invite templates is simple. Add the basic details, set a date and time, and write a short note, then you're ready to start sending. Evite is also an app that makes a difference in the world with its charity option. Using this feature, guests can donate to a nonprofit of your choice.

You can preview invites before sending them to check everything is in order. After sending your invitations, you can communicate with guests in the Messages tab. This is great for sending updates following invites.

Download: Evite for Android | iOS (Free, in-app purchases available)

4. Poply

Close

Poply is a simple and brilliant way to send invites and stay organized for your next event. One of its strongest features is the vertical scrolling invite that lets guests can easily view details one section at a time.

Whether you're sending wedding invites or arranging a casual dinner, Poply offers templates for all kinds of events. Simply select a design, hit Customize, and start entering details. You can also send invites for virtual events, where you can add a link and passcode for any virtual event software.

For those who are more forgetful, you can send event reminders in advance. Managing responses is simple. For every event, Poply shows you who has responded, who has added plus-ones, and who has opened invitations.

Download: Poply for Android | iOS (Free, in-app purchases available)

5. Punchbowl

Close

Punchbowl offers a stress-free way to send invites without getting overwhelmed. All events are categorized on the homepage, including subcategories for specific event types. If your party has a theme, you can head over to the Themes section or use the quick search tool.

The Dashboard helps you manage multiple events at once. Here, you can view invitations and any cards you've sent—an additional feature of Punchbowl. The app also offers some basic event management tools, so you can send invites and plan simultaneously.

Download: Punchbowl for Android | iOS (Free, subscription available)

6. Greetings Island Card Maker

Close

Greetings Island Card Maker offers a unique and simple design system, so you can get creative with your invitation cards. The app offers a range of categories for events, such as graduations, engagements, and baby showers.

For the most personalized designs, you can create custom templates from the Create Your Own tab. Here, you can upload photos, edit frames, and change the background color. Best of all, you can download invitations as a PDF or image file, so you can easily share your invites with friends.

Download: Greetings Island Card Marker for Android | iOS (Free, subscription available)

7. Hub App Party Invitation Maker

Close

Hub makes organizing and planning your next event effortless with its versatile invitation maker. You can create both private and public invitations, and there's a tool for organizing quick meet-ups with friends.

If you're arranging an event as a group, you can set up a poll to get opinions from others. To give extra details, you can send an initial written or voice note along with the invitation. You can also add extra details like a dress code, or whether the event is for adults only.

Guests can respond with three options: Yes, Maybe, and No, giving you a clear idea of how many attendees to expect. Overall, Hub is a brilliant invitation maker for both formal and informal events. Best of all, the app is free to use.

Download: Hub App Party Invitation Maker for Android | iOS (Free)

8. Canva

Close

Canva is a thorough design tool used by many professionals. But you can also use it to create invitations fast and for free. Simply head over to the Templates tab from the sidebar and select Invitations to get started.

While some templates are only available in Canva Pro, you can still access dozens of them in the free version. Canva offers a great depth of design features to decorate your invites, including graphics and motion stickers.

To bring your invitations to life, you can head to the Animate tab. This offers a range of fun and quirky ways to present your information to guests. Finalizing your invites couldn't be easier—simply download your invite or share it on social media.

Download: Canva for Android | iOS (Free, subscription available)

Bring Your Next Event to Life With These Stunning Invitation Apps

Whatever event you're planning, these apps provide a plentiful range of designs to help fit the vibe of what you're planning. An added benefit of using digital invitations over paper ones is you don't need to know everyone's home address. Get creative and try out these digital invite makers.

FAQ

Q: What Is the Best App to Create Invitations?

There isn't only one app to create invitations. There are several apps to choose from to make invitations for your events. Each app comes with its own set of features, and you can go ahead with the one that meets your needs. An app that works for someone may not work for you, and vice versa.

Q: How Do I Make a WhatsApp Invitation Card?

WhatsApp doesn't offer a dedicated option to create invitation cards. You can use other apps available on the market to make invitation cards and use WhatsApp to send those cards to your guests. WhatsApp's built-in options like images, emoji, stickers, and so on, aren't enough to create a proper invitation card.

Q: How Do I Create a Google Invite?

You can create a Google invite using Google Calendar's desktop site, Android app, or iPhone app. You create a new event on your calendar, add your guests, and finish the event to invite people to that specific event on the calendar.
