18 Bright Wall Art Pieces To Warm Up Your Home

No matter the month, we love to surround ourselves with bright hues, neon tones and tropical shades—and it becomes increasingly necessary once the temperatures drop and the sun sets earlier. If you’re anything us, you don’t want to let go of vivid and fun colors even in the dead of winter. So, hanging some bright wall art pieces is one (ridiculously easy) way to keep them in your atmosphere no matter the weather. By decorating your walls with art that boasts the boldest of hues, it will feel like you’re living in a year-round vacation. And isn’t that what we all really want?

Related Stories

Plus, using art prints to decorate your home and make it yours (We love a space that has personality!) is one of the best options for those who constantly want to change things up. Just secure yourself a good set of frames, then switch out bright wall art pieces as often as you like. Use them to create a gallery wall, add a little color over your bed or liven up your kitchen. When you’re tired of a particular piece, you can just change it and add a different one—simple as that. It’s truly the easiest way to never tire of your decor (especially for those who always have the urge to redecorate).

From art prints that feature bright neon images to pieces with a cheeky messages, these vibrant art prints will give new life to any room in your home. If you do have a tendency to want to redecorate your space consistently, make sure you keep this page bookmarked for when you inevitably want to shop new pieces. (We get it.) Until then, this list of some of our favorite bright wall art pieces below should keep you satisfied—for now.

1. Sexy Lips Wall Decor

There’s nothing like a pair of lips to give any room a bold look! The bright yellow background on this lips wall art doesn’t hurt, either. Consider this a showstopper for any home.

2. Yellow Nude Silhouette

Booty booty booty booty, rockin’ everywhere—including your walls! This Yellow Nude Silhouette print from Vintage Book Art Co. is definitely a winner.

3. You Look Good Print

In case you need a reminder every day, hang this Damn, You Look Good art print from Sincerely, Not on any wall in your home. It’s sure to give you the boost of confidence you deserve.

4. Oranges Minimalist Art Print

Get a dose of Vitamin C with this Oranges Minimalist Art Print . It’s the perfect piece to add to a bright gallery wall—or let it stand alone in your kitchen!

5. Mom, I Am A Rich Man Poster

The legend singer and performer Cher once told a story, in which her mom told her to make sure to marry a rich man. She replied, “Mom, I am a rich man“—so you definitely need this poster.

6. Abstract Wall Art Set

Use this set of six abstract art prints as their own miniature gallery wall, or separate them and put them in different spots in your home. Either way, you’re sure to get plenty of compliments.

7. Ice Cream Cones Art Print

Who doesn’t love a good ice cream cone, right? This adorable and bright Ice Cream Cones Art Print is a must-have for any foodie—or anyone who has an affinity for sweet treats.

8. No Bad Vibes Wall Art

Who needs an actual neon sign when you can have this neon sign art print ?! Remind everyone who enters your home that there are no bad vibes allowed.

9. Dance It Out Poster

When in doubt, hang this Dance It Out poster on your wall for an instant mood-booster. It’ll remind you each day to turn on your favorite tunes and bust a move.

10. Abstract & Colorful Home Decor Poster

Fans of bright abstract art , this print is definitely for you. It’s bold, fun and perfect for adding to any wall in your home.

11. Frida Kahlo Quote Art Print

12. Constructed Frame Wall Art

Here’s another bright abstract wall art print for all the contemporary art-lovers out there. This one even comes framed, so you don’t have to worry about finding the perfect home for the print.

13. Party Pups Art Print

OK, this Party Pups Art Print is too cute to resist. If you’re a lover of dogs (Who isn’t?), you definitely need to snag this piece for your home.

14. Interpretive Foliage By André Frey Framed Canvas Wall Art

15. Cheetah Wall Art

16. Spring Art Print by Maheswari Janarthanan

17. Abstract Bright Shapes Illustration

And one more bright abstract art print for good measure. This bold beauty is destined to stand out no matter where in your home you place it.
