Tayla Lynn and Jon Finger

After just two months, Stealing Angels singer Tayla Lynn said "yes" to boyfriend Jon Finger's proposal of marriage. Lynn is the granddaughter of country legend Loretta Lynn, who certainly knows a thing or two about going after what you want. “When you fall in love, you just know," the younger Lynn said in a press release. "Last night the love of my life, with the sweetest, most genuine, heartfelt words a girl could ever dream of, asked me to be his wife. How could there be any other answer? Yes!” Finger dropped to a knee on the beach near his hometown in Seattle, Wash.
