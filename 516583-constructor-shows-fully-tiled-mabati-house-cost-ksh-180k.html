Constructor Shows Off Fully Tiled Mabati House, Says It Cost KSh 180k

  • In a display of innovation and cost-effectiveness, a local constructor @gillydecor caught the attention of many by showcasing a stunning fully tiled mabati house
  • The house boasts a modest price tag of KSh 180,000, and it has captured the hearts of netizens with ost getting awed with it
  • Netizens thronged the constructor's comment section noting that they were super proud of the work that he had done on the modest house

A Kenyan constructor, @gillydecor, has displayed a KSh 180,000 mabati house he built.

Affordable mabati house going for KSH 180k

Mabati, or corrugated iron sheets, have long been a staple in affordable housing solutions across various regions.

The durability, simplicity, and cost-effectiveness of mabati have made it a popular choice for shelter, particularly in areas with limited resources.

Over the years, innovative constructors and architects have sought ways to enhance the utility and aesthetics of mabati houses, turning them into comfortable and stylish abodes.

The unveiling of a fully tiled mabati house on TikTok marked a significant leap forward in this evolution.

By combining the practicality of mabati with the elegance of tiling, this constructor, @gillydecor, showcased the potential to create homes that are not only budget-friendly but also visually appealing.

The constructor revealed that the house cost him KSh 180,000.
