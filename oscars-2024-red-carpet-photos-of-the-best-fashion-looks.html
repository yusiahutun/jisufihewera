Stars Shine on Oscars 2024 Red Carpet: Photos of the Academy Awards Best Fashion Looks

Jimmy Kimmel ​is returning to host the ​2024 Academy Awards, but before the late night host can take the stage, most of Hollywood ​is showing up dressed to impress. Stars from far and wide look to the Oscars to show off their most stunning styles, and 2024 is no exception. ​

Competition was tough this year, but Cillian Murphy’s role in Oppenheimer earned him a Best Actor nomination, and the film itself ​is predicted to take home the win for Best Picture. Emma Stone, who starred in Poor Things, ​is one of the Best Actress nominees and some believe the film might have a chance to beat out Oppenheimer in the Best Picture category.
