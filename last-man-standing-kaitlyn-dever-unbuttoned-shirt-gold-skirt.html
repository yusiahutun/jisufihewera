'Last Man Standing' Star Kaitlyn Dever Stuns Gold Skirt with Super-High Slit

Kaitlyn Dever has become known for her fashionable appearances, and this classic gold-and-black look might be her best yet. The 26-year-old star of Justified, Last Man Standing, Booksmart, and more turned heads at W Magazine's Annual Best Performances Party at Chateau Marmont.

The high-profile event was attended by Hollywood A-listers like Jennifer Lawrence, Kate Hudson, and Jessica Biel, but Kaitlyn stood out among the (gorgeous) crowd and has our vote for best dressed.

Presley Ann

The actress made a statement with a casually cool button-down black shirt that she left unbuttoned paired with a low-slung sparkly gold skirt with a daring slit and matching oversize belt. She paired the look with strappy gold sandals, a single gold chain necklace, and a sleek ponytail.

Presley Ann

Kaitlyn posed with fellow actresses Ella Hunt and Daisy Edgar Jones.

WWD

Kaitlyn once again looked flawless in at a dinner party held at the Sheats-Goldstein Residence. She wore a casual crop top and a black miniskirt with a denim shirt draped over her shoulders and posed for a photo op with Camila Mendes.
