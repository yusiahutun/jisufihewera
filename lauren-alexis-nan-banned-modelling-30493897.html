Boxing influencer who wowed fans in see-through dress was banned from modelling by nan

OnlyFans star turned boxing influencer and Kingpyn host Lauren Alexis was once banned by her nan from modelling.

The 24-year-old stunned fans as she left little to the imagination, wearing an all-black see-through dress while co-hosting Kingpyn’s coverage of Elle Brooke and Daniella Hemsley’s fight in Dublin on Saturday night.

But the brunette beauty previously revealed she had to defy her nan who banned her from modelling before she began building her incredible following on social media today.

The influencer has more than two million followers on Instagram and in excess of 2.4million followers on TikTok, where her popularity has given her the platform to appear on Kingpyn’s boxing productions.
