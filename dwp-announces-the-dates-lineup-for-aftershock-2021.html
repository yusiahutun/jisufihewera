DWP Announces The Dates, Lineup For Aftershock 2021

SAN DIEGO (CelebrityAccess) — Independent festival producer Danny Wimmer Presents announced the dates for return of the Aftershock rock festival with an expanded schedule as a live event in 2021.

Set for October 7 – 10 at Discovery Park in Sacramento, CA, music will kick off on Thursday night with a special warm-up concert presented by Jack Daniel’s.

As previously announced, Aftershock 2021 will be headlined by Metallica, who are lined up to perform two unique sets as well as My Chemical Romance.

The lineup also includes lineup will also include: Limp Bizkit, Rancid, The Offspring, Social Distortion, Rise Against, Volbeat, Machine Gun Kelly, Mastodon, Gojira, SEETHER, Parkway Drive, among others.

Passes for 2021 are on sale now, starting at $119.50 for a single day up to $674.50 for the 4-day VIP experience and can be purchased with a payment plan.

4-Day passes for the festival will be sold in “very limited: quantities and there will be no single day passes sold for Thursday night’s performances.

The past five previous Aftershock festivals have fully sold out.
