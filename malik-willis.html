Malik Willis- Biography

Malik Willis is a college football player from the United States. He is presently a member of the Liberty University football team. He plays the position of Quarterback. He has appeared at the 2018 Music Bowl, the 2020 Cure Bowl, and the 2021 Lending Tree Bowl. According to reports, he intends to enter the NFL draft and believes he will be taken in the first round.

What is the Net Worth of Malik Willis? Salary, Earnings

Malik Willis has made a lot of money from his football career, which he primarily earns via winning games. He could also earn money through brand sponsorships and pre or post-interviews. According to insiders, the specifics of his net worth have been kept confidential and out of the public eye.

Malik Willis- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

Malik Willis was born on May 25, 1999, in Atlanta, Georgia, USA. Harold Willis and Shasta Grier are his parents’ names. Destini Willis is his younger sister. He showed an early interest in participating in a variety of sports. Malik finished his high school education at Westlake High School before transferring to Roswell High School. He attended Auburn University for his undergraduate studies before transferring to Liberty University.

Malik Willis- Professional Career

Malik began his career as a football player in high school. After transferring to Roswell for his senior year, he played and had 2,562 passing yards, 1,033 rushing yards, and 37 total touchdowns. He attended Auburn University in 2017 and 2018. He appeared in 15 games while there. He ran for 309 yards and two scores while completing 11 of 14 throws for 69 yards. Due to NCAA transfer restrictions, he was transferred to Liberty in 2019 and did not play in any games. The following season, he appeared in 10 games and accumulated 2,250 throwing yards with 20 touchdowns and 944 rushing yards with 14 touchdowns. In 2021, he appeared in 13 games and accumulated 2,857 throwing yards and 27 touchdowns, as well as 878 rushing yards and 13 touchdowns.
