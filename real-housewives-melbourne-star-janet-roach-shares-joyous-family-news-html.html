Real Housewives of Melbourne star Janet Roach shares joyous family news

Janet Roach is about to become a grandmother. 

The Real Housewives of Melbourne star has announced that her youngest son, Jake Zogoolas, is expecting his first child. 

'I lost my little dog Bella and was heartbroken for a month — the universe takes away and then gives back,' the 64-year-old told The Herald Sun.

'My pets mean the world to me. She was just a kilo, a tiny little poppet she was. It's a beautiful thing to be expecting a grandchild and we're all very excited.'

Jake and his partner Nicole, are expecting a baby girl in August.
