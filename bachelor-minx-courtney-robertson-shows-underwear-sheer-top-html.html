She stripped naked on The Bachelor to skinny dip with Ben Flajnik.

And reportedly, that was not the first time Courtney Robertson has bared all in front of the cameras as she allegedly once posed nude for a photo shoot.

Amid the rumours, the model showed she has nothing to be ashamed of as she stepped out in a sheer white top that clearly exposed her black bra.

Sheer delight: The Bachelor contestant Courtney Robertson showed her black bra in a white see-through top

Courtney, 28, gave a smile to the cameras as she made her way to a morning photo call in Los Angeles today.

She appeared to be sticking to a sheer theme as she also wore a translucent long black cardigan over her tank top, as well as a pair of skinny jeans.

The reality star completed her off-duty model look with a pair of black boots.

Model behaviour: Courtney had been attending an early morning photo call in Los Angeles

Not so sexy: Courtney then covered up in a brown jumper and red spotted dress for her photo shoot

She then changed into a far less sexy outfit of a brown knitted jumper over a red polka dot chiffon dress and brown boots to shoot some photographs.

And when she was done with her morning behind the camera, Courtney headed off in a red plaid shirt, black leggings and a pair of brown motorcycle boots.

Wearing large sunglasses and with a black quilted bag over one shoulder, Courtney beamed at the awaiting paparazzi as she made her way to her car.

Something to smile about?: Courtney looked pleased as she made her way to film the final episode of The Bachelor over the weekend

She appeared unfazed by the notion that nude photos of her are about to surface, as Star magazine claims to have obtained a cheeky modelling snap of the beauty.

A source told the publication: ‘When Courtney was only 19 she did a nude photo shoot for Abercrombie & Fitch, taken by Bruce Weber.

‘She was young and naïve and she was easily impressed by a photographer like Bruce.’

Vying for his heart: Courtney and Bachelor Ben Flajnik have had a strong connection since the start of the show

Rivals: Courtney will compete against Lindzi Cox in the season finale on Monday

The famous photographer told the magazine that the young models were happy to strip for the sexy shoot.

He said: ‘Courtney is pretty. No one at the shoot was shy, because they all looked great naked, they were young.’

Courtney will compete against Lindzi Cox to win Ben’s heart and the final rose in The Bachelor season finale on Monday.
