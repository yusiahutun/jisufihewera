How David Boreanaz Achieved a Net Worth of $30 Million

David Boreanaz is a renowned American actor who has been active in Hollywood since 1992. David is primarily famous for his role as Angel in the supernatural drama series “Buffy the Vampire Slayer” and its subsequent spin-off “Angel,” nominated for a Golden Globe Award. Although his career journey has not been smooth, David is now a successful actor and television producer with a net worth of over $30 million. In this article, we’ll take a look into his personal life, career, and how he succeeded in building an empire in the film industry.

Who Is David Boreanaz

David was born in Buffalo, New York, on May 16th, 1969. He took his elementary education at Rose Valley Elementary School and pursued his secondary school education at Malvern Preparatory School. He later attended Malvern Preparatory School for two years before graduating. David attended Ithaca College in Ithaca, New York, where he pursued acting and graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in 1991. While in this college, he turned down a full athletic scholarship to attend rigorous acting school. The risk paid off. His mother is a breast cancer survivor, and he has two older sisters.

How Boreanaz Developed a Passion for Acting Career

His father was a police officer, but he died when David was only 11 years old. This tragic incident propelled David to pursue acting to channel his emotions into something positive. During his early days at the Buffalo Academy for Visual and Performing Arts, David had already caught the attention of several theater producers and directors. His first career was as a cameraman at a local station in Pennsylvania. According to Celebrity Net Worth, the actor began his career with his TV debut in 1993 when he took part in Married…With Children. However, this didn’t propel him to stardom because most people thought of him as an extra.

His big breakthrough came in 1997 when he landed the role of a young vampire called Angel in Buffy the Vampire Slayer. He has also been featured in movies such as Suffering Man’s Charity and Valentine. His first significant acting role was in Buffy the Vampire Slayer, where he played Angel. Angel was a vampire with a soul, and he fell in love with Buffy. The series lasted for seven seasons, from 1997 to 2003. In 2001, David Boreanaz got his first starring role on television as FBI agent Seeley Booth on the show Bones. In addition to acting, David Boreanaz has been active in producing films and television shows. He co-founded Underground Films with Joss Whedon and is a partner in Fuse Entertainment, a production company. He also formed a partnership with Mark Gordon to create Boreanaz Pictures, which has produced shows such as Angel and Bones. Boreanaz directed an episode of Blade’s short-lived television series adaptation titled “Conviction.” The show was canceled after 13 episodes due to low ratings. In 2011, he made his feature film directorial debut with Alter Egos.

Films and TV Shows That David Boreanaz Took Part In

Besides playing a part in dozens of films, he is an executive producer in television series like Buffy the Vampire Slayer, where he received a Teen Choice Award in 1999 and a PRISM Award in 2011 for Bones. His professional career has shown no signs of slowing down. He is still actively acting and has been a part of several hit shows, including Bones, Hawaii Five-0, Blue Bloods and many more. Since 2017 he has starred as Jason Hayes on the series SEAL Team. Boreanaz has also directed several episodes of Bones and SEAL Team. In the early 2000s, Boreanaz was one of TV’s highest-paid actors per episode; however, he didn’t keep every cent. He helped pay for his mother Judy’s cancer treatments and donated to various charities.

How Much Did He Earn per Episode?

Boreanaz is an accomplished TV producer and director. According to Celebrity FM, he was paid $65,000 per episode in his initial season of Buffy the Vampire Slayer. He earned a reported $250,000 per episode as an actor in Bones and $100,000 per episode in Angel. At one point, he nabbed a 15% slice of the action and producer fees on the back end for Bones. The actor pulls in a steady paycheck with steady work on television.

Marriage and Family Life

David Boreanaz has achieved a lot of success in his career, but the fact that he’s been able to balance his personal and professional life is even more admirable. Boreanaz first got married to Ingrid Quinn in 1997 and later in 1999. He later got married to Jaime Bergman in 2001, and they have two children. A son named Jaden Rayne was born in 2002, and a daughter named Bella Vita was born in 2009. Besides being a family man, David is a great hockey fan whose efforts are felt by the Philadelphia Flyers, whom he supports.

David’s Charitable Work

There are fewer celebrities who are more charitable than David Boreanaz. According to CBS, He actively supports Animal Hope and Wellness Foundation. Also, he works closely with charitable organizations in conjunction with World Vision, Save the Children, The Creative Coalition, United Nations Foundation, and PETA.

Awards and Winnings

Throughout his acting career, David Boreanaz has garnered many awards and nominations. For his portrayal of Angel in the series, he won a Satellite Award for Best Actor in a Television Series, Musical or Comedy. David’s name has appeared in several nominations for various prestigious awards that include a Golden Globe award, three Screen Actors Guild awards, seven Primetime Emmy Award nominations. Another of David Boreanaz’s most notable roles was as Seeley Booth on Bones, which garnered him three People’s Choice Awards; one for Favorite TV Crime Drama Actor (2007) and two for Favorite TV Crime Drama (2008 and 2010). He has been nominated for four Saturn Awards, three ALMA Awards, two Teen Choice Awards, and two Screen Actors Guild Awards.

Conclusion

David Boreanaz is a self-made actor and television producer whose journey started as an extra character in 1993. Over the period, he furnished his skills and became a great actor, and now he is a famous television producer. With over 20 stirring roles and appearances on dozens of series, he has amassed his wealth into a net worth of over $30 million dollars. David is now among the celebrated actors in Hollywood, looked upon by other artists for inspiration.
