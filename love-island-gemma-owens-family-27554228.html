Gemma Owen's family reveal unexpected 'long term ambition' after Love Island

Gemma Owen's family have revealed what her "long term ambition" is.

The Love Island star, 19, has made quite the impact on fans since she entered the villa on day one, and has left them wanting to know everything about her.

Taking to her Instagram stories with a "Friday facts - Gemma addition," the ITV2 stars handlers revealed several unknown facts about the brunette beauty - including that her long term ambition is to own her own beach club.
