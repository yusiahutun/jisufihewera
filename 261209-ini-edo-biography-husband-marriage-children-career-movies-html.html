Ini Edo biography

Ini Edo is a Nigerian native who loved acting from a tender age. She was born on April 23rd, 1982 in Akwa Ibom, and grew up in Calabar. She comes from a very humble background. Her parents raised her in a strictly religious environment. Her mother was a school teacher and her father a church elder.

Siblings

The actress considers herself the second born in a family of four; an elder cousin who she loves as an elder sister. She also has a younger brother and sister. There has been news going around about Ini Edo twin sister.

Esther Urra is a Nigerian who has a striking resemblance to Ini. This has earned her the twin sister title. She is from Oturkpo Local Government of Benue State. Esther studied at the Benue State University.

Education

The talented actress attended the Calabar college, Cornelius Connely. After graduating, she joined the University of Uyo in her home state where she attained a diploma in Theatre Arts.

Ini later advanced her education and pursued a degree in English at the same university. In 2014, she was awarded a scholarship to study Law at the National Open University of Nigeria.

Marriage

In November 2008, Ini married a Nigerian American-based entrepreneur, Phillip Ehiagwina. The two were joined in a church ceremony in Nigeria. In February 2009, the two held a second wedding ceremony in Houston, Texas where Ini Edo husband was based.

However, six years later, in 2014, the couple went separate ways. Ini cited irreconcilable differences as the reason for the breakup. There were rumours that she had cheated on him. Others claimed that she had 'snatched' him from Ruth Okoro, his ex-wife.

Children

Netizens are frequently looking for Ini Edo kids online. The actress, however, does not have any children yet. It was rumoured that childlessness was one of the reasons her marriage ended, but the actress has since clarified that this was not true.

She gave thanks to God for blessing her with a baby, which was a miracle to them after several miscarriages. Career

Ini Edo's career kicked off shortly after she had graduated from the university. She tried her hand in different auditions until the year 2003 when she starred in her maiden movie, ''Thick Madam.'' However, her second movie ''World Apart'' (2004) is the one that propelled her to fame. She has starred in over 100 Nollywood movies since.

Ini is one of the highest-paid and sought after actresses in Nigeria, with a net worth of approximately $3 million. The actress has a mansion in Lekki, Lagos. Ini Edo house is worth approximately $183, 000. Her beauty and talent have made her a force to reckon with in Nollywood.

Besides acting, the talented movie star is also a brand ambassador and has endorsed brands such as Slim tea Nigeria, Globacom, Chivita, and Noble Hair. In 2011, the actress was appointed by the United Nations Habitat youth envoys director as a youth ambassador. She was appointed alongside Ramsey Nouah and Vikram Vinod.

Ini has also had opportunities to serve in global capacities. In 2013, she was a judge at the Miss Black beauty contest, United Kingdom. Other judges on the panel were Jones Adebayo, Sporah Njau, Charles Ven, Justine Mutale, and Jenni Steele. Ini is also a philanthropist who spends much of her time giving back to the less privileged.

More recently, ''the pearl of Nollywood,'' as some have called her, was appointed the Special Assistant to the Governor of Akwa Ibom on Culture, Entertainment, and Tourism. She has held this position since 2016. Ini has been excited about serving in this capacity, more specifically in her home town.
