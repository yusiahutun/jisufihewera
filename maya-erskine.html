Biography Timeline

Maya Erskine is an American actress and writer known for her work in film and television. She is best known for co-creating and starring in the Hulu comedy series “PEN15”, for which she has received critical acclaim. Here is a detailed biography timeline of Maya Erskine:

  • 2005-2009: Erskine attends New York University’s Tisch School of the Arts, earning a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Theatre.
  • 2010: Erskine makes her television debut in a “The Electric Company” episode.
  • 2012: Erskine appears in the film “Betrayed” and the television series “Made in Jersey”.
  • 2013: Erskine appears in the film “The Longest Week” and the television series “Man Seeking Woman”.
