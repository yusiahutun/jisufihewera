Unga at KSh 180? Spot Check Shows 2kg Packet Retails at Average Price of KSh 195

  • Speaking during the launch of the Nairobi River Commission, the head of state suggested a 2kg packet of maize meal had reduced to KSh 180
  • While responding to Azimio la Umoja One Kenya Coalition Party leader Raila Odinga's call to lower the cost of living, Ruto said he had managed to bring down the cost of unga
  • A spot check by TUKO.co.ke revealed that the president was right to say the price of unga had reduced but not to KSh 180

On Thursday, February 23, President William Ruto said his government had stabilised food prices in Kenya amid the high cost of living.

Speaking during the launch of the Nairobi River Commission, the head of state suggested a 2kg packet of maize meal had reduced to KSh 180.

While responding to Azimio la Umoja One Kenya Coalition Party leader Raila Odinga's call to lower the cost of living, Ruto said he had managed to bring down the cost of unga.

"You people (Raila and retired president Uhuru Kenyatta) had five years in government with the handshake. During that period the price of unga shot to KSh 220. Now we’ve brought it down to KSh 180. Give us time and we shall bring it down to KSh 140 and KSh 120. They should stop ordering us around because they had their time and failed to utilise it,” the president said.

Unga's average price in Kenya

A spot check by TUKO.co.ke revealed that the president was right to say the price of unga had reduced for normal brands.

However, the average price of the commodity is at KSh 195 and not KSh 180.

On Naivas Supermarket's website, for instance, a 2kg packet of unga retails at between KSh 181 and KSh 212 depending on the brand.

The giant retailer sells Jogoo maize meal at KSh 181, Soko at KSh 194, Mama (KSh 198), Dola (KSh 212) Pembe (KSh 208).

On the other hand premium brands such as Amaize, Raha and Hostess retail at over KSh 200.

A 2kg packet of Amaze premium maize meal goes for KSh 237, Hostess costs KSh 200 while Raha premium maize meal is sold at KSh 262 for the same quantity.

At Quickmart Supermarket, a 2kg packet of Pembe maize meal retails at KSh 202, Jogoo (KSh 186), Soko (KSh 185), Amaize (KSh 227) and Hostess (KSh 216).

William Ruto promises to lower unga prices

Kenyans could soon purchase a 2 kg packet of maize flour at KSh 120 after months of grappling with exorbitant prices.

The commodity's price had hit KSh 230 for a single packet with Kenyans decrying the unbearable state of things.

As per the head of state, a packet of flour will soon drop to KSh 120.
