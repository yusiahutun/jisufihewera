Dodd promoted at First State Bank | Business

Joel C. Clements, chairman, CEO and president of First State Bank in Waynesboro, announced that Darron Dodd has been promoted to senior vice president in Laurel.

Dodd

Dodd is a graduate of the University of Mississippi and the Graduate School of Banking at Louisiana State University. He is a past president and current board member of the Sertoma Club of Laurel. He also serves as the treasurer for Jones County Ducks Unlimited. Dodd has been a local T-ball coach for more than 20 years and is currently coaching a local soccer team.

Dodd and his wife Lindsay have two children Clark and Blair. They attend Westminster Presbyterian Church, where he serves as deacon.

Since 1934, First State Bank has been committed to meeting the financial needs of area individuals and businesses, all while providing superior customer service.

Headquartered in Waynesboro, they now have locations in Ellisville, Laurel, Leakesville, Lucedale, Meridian and Quitman. First State Bank has earned a 5-star rating from Bauer Financial for 103 consecutive quarters, qualifying them for “Best of Bauer” status.

For more on First State Bank, visit www.firststatebnk.com.
