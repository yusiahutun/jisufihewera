Eleanor Calder 'flew to Australia to try and save relationship' with Louis Tomlinson

    Louis Tomlinson and his ex-girlfriend Eleanor Calder were apparently close to splitting for a while, and it has now emerged that even a flight out to Australia to visit the One Direction band member couldn't save their relationship.

    Eleanor, 22, is said to have jetted out to Sydney to visit her boyfriend Louis while he was on tour with his fellow bandmates Harry Styles, Liam Payne, Niall Horan and Zayn Malik.

    The pretty brunette flew the 20,000 miles to reportedly work through the couple's "differences" – in a bid to save their four-year relationship.

    Eleanor Calder flew out to visit Louis Tomlinson, but they "couldn't work through their differences"

    "Cracks were showing for a while. It's incredibly tough with the boys being on the road so much," a source told the . "They had a month off over Christmas but since then they have been in Australia and the Far East."

    "It put a real strain on the relationship," the insider added. "But even after Eleanor flew out to visit Louis, they couldn't work through their differences.

    "Weeks later, when they were on opposite sides of the world again, they called it a day."
