Lemon Goat Cheese Dip Recipe

Andrew Purcell

Read More

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Yields: 12 serving(s)

Prep Time: 10 mins

Total Time: 10 mins

Cal/Serv: 240

Ingredients

Save Recipe
  • 1 c.

    mayonnaise

  • 1

    (10 1/2-ounce) goat-cheese log, crumbled

  • 2 tbsp.

    olive oil

  • 2

    green onions, chopped

  • 1 tbsp.

    fresh lemon juice (about 1/2 lemon)

  • 1 tsp.

    lemon zest (about 1/2 lemon)

  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper

  • Pita chips, for serving

Directions

  • Step 1In a food processor, combine first 6 ingredients, plus 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1 teaspoon pepper; blend until smooth. Transfer to a bowl and season with salt and pepper. Serve with pita chips.

Tips & Techniques

Tip: Substitute low-fat Greek yogurt for mayo and save 119 calories, 14 grams fat, and 10 milligrams cholesterol per serving.
