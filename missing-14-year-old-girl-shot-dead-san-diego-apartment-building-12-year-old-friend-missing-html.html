Anna Hernandez found shot dead in San Diego but Nelly Espinosa is still missing

San Diego police have recovered the decomposing body of a 14-year-old girl who went missing with her 12-year-old friend earlier this month. 

The victim, Anna Hernandez, was shot according to San Diego Police Department. 

The youngster's body was recovered from a canyon behind an apartment complex in Market Street.

Her 12-year-old friend Nelly Espinosa is still missing. Ms Hernandez's body was found very close to Ms Espinosa's house. 

SDPD Lieutenant Mike Hastings told KTLA Ms Hernandez was beaten and shot a number of times in the upper chest.

Her body was wrapped in a blanket. 

Friends and relatives have erected a make-shift shrine near the site where the youngster's body was dumped. 

A close friend Karla Chavez said: 'I can’t believe this is real, she was only 14.' 

Both girls were last seen on November 13.
