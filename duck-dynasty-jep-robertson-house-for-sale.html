'Duck Dynasty' Star Selling His Louisiana Estate [Pictures]

The buyer of Jep Robertson's 6,500 square-foot Louisiana home will get a rustic palace as charming as it is famous. There's character in every room, and characters in several neighboring houses.

The Houston Chronicle points out that the West Monroe, La., house is on "Robertson Row," where several of the Duck Dynasty stars currently reside. They come at no extra cost when you fork over the $1.4 million asking price. The listed amenities at Realtor.com show six bedrooms, five full bathrooms and one half bathroom, shiplap ceilings, hardwood floors, an outdoor kitchen, music room and more.

While it was only built in 1972, the home feels historic with shabby-chic furnishings and a huge copper tub in the master bathroom. The Arcadian style home has exposed brick and100-year-old beams, and it's been on the market since August. The lakeside property also features beehives, gardens, a chicken coop and a fruit orchard.

Click through some of the above photos to get a glimpse at the Robertsons' double living rooms, historic fireplaces, bedrooms and more. The couple and their kids recently moved to Austin Texas to launch his food truck.
