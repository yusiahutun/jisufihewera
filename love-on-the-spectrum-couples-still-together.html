Love on the Spectrum Couples Still Together in 2024: Where Are They Now?

The Emmy Award-winning reality show is back. Here are all the Love On The Spectrum couples still together after the two seasons of the Australian version and the US version.

The US show’s synopsis is as follows: “Following the success of the multi-award-winning Australian series, this US-based series tells the stories of a unique and diverse cast of characters searching for something we all hope to find — love.”

Creator Cian O’Clery described how they made the show on Netflix to Tudum. He mentioned that he met a lot of people who had autism while he was working with Love on the Spectrum co-executive producer Karina Holden. “Not only did we get to know the people we were filming really well, but we also spoke to hundreds and hundreds of people on the spectrum about their lives and what was important to them. The story of people wanting to find love and wanting to date just kept coming up. To us, that was something that wasn’t right. There was obviously a lack of support, and it just felt like a really interesting area to shine a light on.”

He continued, “The other thing that we noticed was the diversity of the spectrum and just how different people can be. That’s the most important thing that we can do with this series, to show that huge diversity that people don’t really understand. I should note, as well, it’s really important to acknowledge that not everybody who is on the spectrum needs support in the world of dating and relationships. Everyone’s so different. But a lot of people do, and a lot of people we spoke to did and were really calling out for that.”

A couple of familiar faces will be returning to the show for a second chance at love, and some new faces will make their debut. So which Love On The Spectrum couples are still together? Read more to find out.

  • Abbey & David

    Season: 1 —US

    Abbey and David are still together after Love On The Spectrum USA. In fact, they plan to showcase their love in season 2 of the Netflix show.

    In a cute Instagram post, they shared their affection for each other. “Classic Abbey & David, with his milk and her Chardonnay ✨ David is truly such an amazing caring boyfriend. Abbey was struggling with a crying baby at a restaurant at the next table. Every time the baby screamed David covered Abbey’s ears to help her deal with the painful nature of that sound. We love how he understands her needs when it comes to her autism and sensory input.”

  • Ronan & Katie

    Season: 2 — Australia

    Ronan and Katie hit it off after their picnic date. Together, they had sweet dates like going to the arcade. Since their appearance on the show, Ronan frequently posts their activities together.

    “Can’t believe it’s been three years since we met on Love on the Spectrum,” he posted in November 2023. “We’ve had so many incredible and romantic dates together! I can’t wait to have more amazing adventures with Katie. 💕 💕”

  • Ruth & Thomas

    Season: 2 — Australia

    Ruth and Thomas are not only together after their appearance on Love On The Spectrum Australia, but they are happily married! Shortly after the end of the show, the couple moved in together in a family of 9 as they combined their pets together. Ruth announced their marriage in an Instagram made on May 2020.
