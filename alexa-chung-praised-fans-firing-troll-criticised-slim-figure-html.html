Alexa Chung praised by fans for firing back at troll who criticised her slim figure

Alexa Chung has been lauded by her fans for blasting a troll who criticised her slim figure.

The 35-year-old star was quick to fire back with a simple yet effective retort when a fan left a cruel comment on her latest Instagram post shared on Thursday.

The fashion icon was sporting a 60s inspired shift dress with a short hemline and flared sleeves, her long legs taking centre stage in the image.

Alexa replied: Speaking of obsolete trends, didn’t bullying people on social media get cancelled?'

The model turned designer's fans praised her for her quick-witted comeback, commenting 'YASSS' and 'She handled that perfectly!'

Others chimed in with statements defending Alexa, with one follower posting: 'interesting that you think making unsolicited personal comments about someone else’s appearance, making assumptions about someone else’s life, then telling them to ‘go home’ isn’t bullying...'

Another fan wrote: 'U think she should stop showing herself just for having natural skinny, and healthy body? Plus she does not base her feed on a body image, just her style. Let her and her cleverness flow.'

Alexa previously revealed she was upset about people commenting on her weight online, saying: ‘I think it's about time people stopped judging women on their appearance and more on their intellect.

She told Fashionista.com it ‘sucked’ that she had been dubbed a ‘thinspiration' - as she insisted she would like to gain 10 pounds.

She said: ‘How do you know I’m not looking in the mirror and going ‘I wish I could gain ten pounds?’ Which is actually quite often the case.

‘But if you say that you sound like you’re bragging that you’re naturally thin, and you’re not allowed to do that because even though it’s not the ideal weight, it kind of is as well.’
