10 movies like How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days for romance comedy fans

If you have a soft spot for romantic comedy movies, chances are you've already indulged in the charms of How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days. And if they do, which ones are the best?

How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days is a 2003 romantic comedy film centring on the escapades of Ben Berry, a ladies' man and Andie Anderson, a magazine columnist. The plot revolves around a bet made by the ladies' man: he wagers with his friends that he can make a woman fall in love with him within ten days.

Unknown to him, the woman he's courting is a magazine columnist on a mission. She is crafting a new article, How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days, and is determined to use every tactic to drive her new boyfriend into madness.
