Opinion: Taylor Swift's 'boyfriend' Travis is playing the part of future husband 'All Too Well'

From Heartbreak Princess to Pop Nation’s sweetheart, Taylor Swift’s new ‘boyfriend’ Travis Kelce has caught everyone’s eye and rumors say that the couple is getting too serious too soon.

There’s no doubt that Travis Kelce has the potential to be ‘the one.’ From spending millions in revamping his life to sending flashy bouquets, we say that the NFL celebrity is perfect future husband material, and he knows it ‘All Too Well.’

Taylor Swift’s ‘boyfriend’ Travis has been talking about the singer for a while

Within months of meeting each other which was not completely known to the world, Travis and Taylor Swift ‘introduced’ their new-found connection in a cinematic manner.

After attending a game of Kansas City Chiefs at Arrowhead Stadium, Travis took the music legend and vanished into the unknown in his ‘Getaway Car,’ only to relish the moment later at his podcast with his brother Jason Kelce showing how clearly smitten he was.

But before even meeting the hitmaker he spoke about how he wanted to meet her.

Beginning with a friendship bracelet

While many think that Taylor and Travis are moving ‘too soon,’ the NFL star showed all the signs that he was ‘serious’ about Tay Tay even before she attended his match on September 24.

Travis attended the Eras tour in July and even wanted to meet Taylor on the day. While attending the concert, he hoped to give her a friendship bracelet with his number on it. While his friends thought he was joking, a source close to the celebrity revealed he was ‘serious’ about liking Taylor all along.

The ‘supportive’ boyfriend

Travis attention to detail when it comes to the Daylight crooner is seriously impressive. On their first ‘public’ date, Travis wore a 1989 Bedroom Painting’ matching set piece as he left the Arrowhead stadium. Some fans believe it was to show support for Taylor’s album 1989 Taylor’s Version which was released on October 27.

Later, Taylor also started wearing a couple of items as eagle-eyed fans noticed a 87 bracelet on the Blank Space crooner’s hand as she attended another Kansas City Chiefs match. This is the number on Trav’s jersey.

Chivalry is not dead

He is also showing that he is an old-school gentleman when it comes to treating his lady right. His chivalry shows that he is cherishing his bond with the Lover crooner.

From opening car doors and ushering the bodyguard out of the way to keeping his hands on Tay Tay in a protective manner, Travis has made Swifties swoon over the duo’s fairytale romance.

Body language enthusiasts have also noticed the particular way Travis holds Taylor’s hands, almost clasping them with his own, a tell-tale sign of his growing fondness.

The ritual of big gestures for Traylor started early in their relationship as reports claimed Travis splashed out a whopping 2.5k bouquet to send Taylor at the premiere of Eras Tour film as he could not attend the red carpet in person.

Taylor Swift’s new ‘boyfriend’ has also apparently signed up to buy a new mansion for ‘privacy’.

A full makeover has begun for Taylor Swift’s new ‘boyfriend’

Along with his gestures, Travis has also started a full-fledged makeover of his life in an attempt to woo his new lady love. The NFL star has bought a house that is worth almost $6 million in Kansas City.

Sources close to the celebrity couple have claimed that Travis made Taylor a part of the house-buying decision as they toured houses together.

“Travis loves Taylor being at his games and hanging out in his city, and they’ve talked about both wanting to have a steady home base,” a source said. “They toured houses together before he bought it.”

Tay Tay’s new beau has also filed for five new trademarks of popular phrases including his own name and his social media handle, KillaTrav.

It seems like Travis is entering his money-making era to keep up with his ‘billionaire’ girlfriend before taking the next big step.

Speaking of Travis’ true future plans, a source revealed: “They’re looking forward to spending the holidays together, too. His schedule is crazy during football season, and she’s got more concerts coming up, but they’re working around all that.”

“Family rituals”

As the holiday seasons arrive, Taylor and Travis are also planning to start their own family rituals. An insider says the You Belong With Me crooner is trying “to find a way to host a big Thanksgiving dinner with both of their families,” adding, “She says she’s ready to start traditions.”

It is THAT serious.

The source revealed: “They’re already making plans for the future.”

After searching for a while, Taylor has found the guy she has been waiting for.

They added: “He and Taylor have even talked about kids. Travis wants them in the next year or so, and Taylor, of course, is all in. He’s exactly the guy she’s been waiting for.”

Looks like Taylor Swift has found The 1 in her new ‘boyfriend’. We will wait to hear more about their plans of leaving the Christmas lights up ’til January soon!
