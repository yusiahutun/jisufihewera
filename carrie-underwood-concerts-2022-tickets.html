Carrie Underwood Concerts 2022: How can I buy tickets?

GRAMMY award-winning artist Carrie Underwood is holding performances through 2023.

Her concerts come after she appeared at the Stagecoach Festival in early May 2022.

If you click on a link in this story we may earn affiliate revenue.

When is Carrie Underwood's tour?

Carrie Underwood is going on her Denim & Rhinestones tour which kicks off in Greenville, South Carolina on October 15, 2022.

Her performances will continue through the beginning of 2023 and will conclude on March 17 in Seattle, Washington.

In a news release on her website, Underwood said, “I’m thrilled to be hitting the road again with THE DENIM & RHINESTONES TOUR."

She added, "I’m excited to bring the new music of Denim & Rhinestones to life on tour, as well as put new spins on familiar favorites.

"We’ve been working hard already preparing an amazing show, and I can’t wait to see everyone on the road!”

The tour comes after the release of Underwood's newest album, Denim & Rhinestones, which is set to be available on June 10.

Those wishing to purchase the album can do so on Underwood's official website.

Where is Carrie Underwood performing?

Underwood headlined at the Stagecoach Festival with surprise guest Axl Rose on May 2, 2022, and her upcoming tour will continue to take her across the US.

Her tour dates include:

  • October 17 at Gainbridge Fieldhouse (Indianapolis, Indiana)
  • October 18 at Van Andel Arena (Grand Rapids, Michigan)
  • October 20 at Rupp Arena (Lexington, Kentucky)
  • October 22 at Allstate Arena (Rosemont, Illinois)
  • October 23 at Fiserv Forum (Milwaukee, Wisconsin)
  • October 25 at Target Center (Minneapolis, Minnesota)
  • October 27 at Alerus Center (Grand Forks, North Dakota)
  • October 31 at BOK Center (Tulsa, Oklahoma)
  • November 2 at Moody Center (Austin, Texas)
  • November 3 at Toyota Center (Houston, Texas)

  • November 7 at Enterprise Center (St. Louis, Missouri)

  • November 19 at Chase Center (San Francisco, California)

For the list of tour dates and locations for 2023, fans can visit Carrie Underwood's website.

How can I buy tickets?

Tickets for Underwood's performances can be purchased on her website, with prices starting at $20 for her Las Vegas residency and going up to $500 for her July 7 concert.

Fans can also purchase tickets on Ticketmaster, StubHub, and VividSeats.
