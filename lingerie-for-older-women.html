Why women over 50 should wear lingerie

While you might think you’re too old for lingerie, think again. Plus, there’s even psychological research that reveals how selecting the pair of underwear we wear can affect our mood.

Even if lingerie isn’t showing, wearing it underneath our clothes can be a confidence booster. “It’s like a sexy little secret we keep under our ‘Clark Kent’ uniforms, waiting to break out and be the superwomen we are,” adds Yemini. And there are also plenty of options that can help boost confidence in the bedroom or make you feel good to even wear while snoozing.

Additionally, lingerie can actually provide shape and extra support for your assets, meaning pieces are no longer just for looks. That’s because the latest lingerie styles have built-in cups (without painful underwire!), shapewear, adjustable straps, velcro fastening and more that help older women feel comfortable showing off some skin.

Full coverage lingerie can still be sexy and comfortable

Even though “our bodies change in so many subtle ways as we age,” notes Arias, there’s no need to sacrifice comfort or coverage when wearing or picking out lingerie. As we get older “we often want to hide these changes and start to gravitate towards full-coverage lingerie,” she says. Like trading “thongs for granny panties — but full coverage doesn’t have to mean big cotton granny panties,” she insists.

There are so many different materials to choose from that still give you full coverage, but look and feel a little more alluring than your regular cotton pair of panties. For instance, take silk or lace. Swapping out your old underwear for ones with these details automatically will make you feel more elevated, confident and sexy. And because these fabrics feel like a second skin, you’ll feel extra comfortable to boot.
