Donny Osmond Returns To Touring After 10 Years

Donny Osmond returns to the road in May for his first solo tour in more than 10 years in support of his new CD, This is the Moment.

Backed by a 5-piece rhythm section and 14-piece orchestra, "An Evening With Donny Osmond" will stop at approximately 20 North American theatres in May and June with several multiple-night engagements. The tour then heads overseas for a three-month tour of Europe, Asia, and Australia.

The tour's second U.S. leg resumes in January 2002 with dates through the spring. The William Morris Agency is booking the tour.
