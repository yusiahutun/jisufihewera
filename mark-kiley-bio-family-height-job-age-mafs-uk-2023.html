Mark Kiley On MAFS UK 2023

Mark Kiley joined MAFS UK as one of the eight “intruder bride/groom” to spice things up. Customer service manager Mark, who lives in London, is one of the ‘intruders’ and is trying to find his Mr. Right with the aid of the MAFS UK specialists.

His bio states: “Fashion savvy” Sean is a social butterfly who loves a party, but just don’t get on his wrong side. Sean’s friends nicknamed him “Brutal Barry” for his outspoken and honest opinion which has landed him in trouble previously. When it comes to love, Sean came out later in life and has admitted that feeling “misunderstood and confused” led to years of him missing out on the gay dating scene. He’s never had “anything near a relationship”, but now in his thirties, he’s ready to commit – with the experts’ help!”

Before appearing on MAFS, he appeared on Channel 4’s other dating show Dates Back in 2017. He went on a blind date with Junior on the show, “I want Mark and Junior to get married and also be my best friends.” Simply said, it wasn’t meant to be, and Mark later disclosed that Junior and he had split up soon after the show aired.
