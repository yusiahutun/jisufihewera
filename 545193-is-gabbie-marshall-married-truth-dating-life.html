Who is Gabbie Marshall?

Gabbie Marshall is one of the best and most senior players on Iowa's basketball team. She joined the prestigious basketball team in 2019 – 2020.

Marshall was born to Ernest and Marne Marshall on August 22, 2000. Her parents played basketball for various teams. Ernest played college basketball for Bellarmine University, and her mother played for Aquinas College.

Gabbie started playing basketball for Mount Notre Dame High School in Cincinnati, Ohio in high school. During her reign, the team won state titles in 2017 and 2019. Gabbie’s team was also named All-State three times.

Gabbie’s impressive court skills allowed her to join Iowa’s Hawkeyes. Even though she was a freshman in 2019-2020, she played in every game and got a chance to join the starting line-up next season.

Her entry into the starting line-up was worthwhile, as she led the team to the 2023 NCAA title game alongside star point guard Caitlin Clark.
