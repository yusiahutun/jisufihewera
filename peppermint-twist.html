How to Make Peppermint Twist

Crushed candy cane and peppermint schnapps make this pretty-in-pink drink even more creamy cool.

Serving Size:

2

Active Time:

15 min.

Total Time:

15 min.

Ingredients

  • 4 oz. vodka
  • 1 Tbs. finely crushed candy cane
  • ¼ tsp. mint extract
  • 4 oz. peppermint schnapps
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 2 oz. heavy cream
  • Red liquid food coloring
  • Candy canes
  • Mint sprigs

Instructions

  • In cocktail shaker, combine vodka, crushed candy cane and extract; muddle until candy cane dissolves and flavors blend.
  • Add schnapps, ice, cream and tiny drop of food coloring; cover. Shake until well chilled, about 30 sec. Divide between glasses. Garnish with candy canes and mint springs.
