Wendy Williams Reveals She's 'Not on the Market Anymore' -- But Isn't Dating 27-Year-Old!

Her new beau? He's a doctor.

Wendy Williams returned from a five-week break from her talk show on Monday morning and made herself the Hot Topic of the morning with some big news: she's got a new man.

While Williams was photographed all over New York City with 27-year-old Marc Tomblin during her hiatus, she insists he's not who she's currently dating.

"I'm not on the market anymore," she revealed, getting both giddy and emotional as she spoke to her studio audience. "I'm not in love. I'm not in love ... but there's somebody that I'm crazy about."
