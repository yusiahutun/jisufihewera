Airelles Val d'Isere Hotel Review, France

Val d'Isere, Rhône-Alpes, France

After a rocky pre-pandemic debut, Airelles Val d’Isère has most definitely found its mountain mojo. With its spectacular slope-side setting, impeccable service and playful mix of medieval themed interiors served with a refreshingly contemporary twist, it adds an extra dimension to the resort’s five-star hotel scene.
