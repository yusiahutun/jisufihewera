Quick Facts: Meagan Fitzgerald

  • Meagan Fitzgerald was born in Princeton Junction, New Jersey.
  • She studied at Howard University where she obtained a Bachelor's Degree in Broadcast journalism. 
  • While attending Howard University, she was an intern in News4's Consumer Unit.
  • She played lacrosse for Howard University's Division 1 program.
  • The reporter also served as the head coach for the women's lacrosse team at the University of San Diego.
  • In November 2008, she began working as an anchor and reporter for news channel KTVE/KARD-TV.
  • Three years later, she joined KUSA-TV in January 2012, where she served as a news reporter and fill-in anchor for three years and three months.
  • In May 2019, she joined NBC Washington.
  • Since 2019, she is working as a co-anchor for Sunday NEWS4 Today.
  • She a Rotarian and a member of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority and the National Association of Black Journalists.
