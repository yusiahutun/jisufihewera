Zayn Malik developed 'serious' eating disorder during One Direction days

Former One Direction star Zayn Malik has revealed he developed a 'serious' eating disorder during his five year stint in the boyband.

The 23-year-old Pillowtalk hitmaker is set to release his eponymous autobiography in which he discusses the terrifying condition in which he reveals he was not concerned about his weight but merely attempting to gain control. 

In quotes obtained by The Sun, Zayn candidly admits he made himself extremely ill and would often go up to three days straight without consuming any food after the strains of the job badly affected his eating patterns.

Zayn left One Direction in March 2015 in a move which stunned the legion of adoring admirers the group had garnered since shooting to fame on X Factor in 2010. 

Last year, he released his shock statement reading: 'I have to do what feels right in my heart. I am leaving because I want to be a normal 22-year-old who is able to relax and have some private time out of the spotlight.'

Now the Bradford-born singer, who has since enjoyed huge solo success, has shed light on some of the troubles attached to his departure - most shockingly his eating disorder. 

He penned in the book: 'When I look back at images of myself from around November 2014, I can see how ill I was. Something I’ve never talked about in public before, but which I have come to terms with since leaving the band, is that I was suffering from an eating disorder.

Zayn has been somewhat embattled since leaving the band as he also suffers from an anxiety disorder which has hindered his solo work. 

Last month he was forced to pull out of a gig in Dubai next month due to his ongoing anxiety surrounding solo performances, it was revealed on Tuesday.

He was due to perform at the Autism Rocks Arena however he revealed his crippling anxiety has forced him to withdraw from the performance as he is not 'sufficiently confident'.

Back in June, the pop star pulled out of playing the Capital Summertime Ball in London just 90 minutes before the show was due to kick off, as he was suffering the same issue - which he explained was persisting.

After his One Direction departure, Zayn released his chart-topping debut solo single Pillowtalk from his album Mind Of Mine.

While he has been inundated with praise for his solo work, Zayn - who has been dating supermodel Gigi Hadid, 21, since late 2015 - admitted he is struggling to work alone.
