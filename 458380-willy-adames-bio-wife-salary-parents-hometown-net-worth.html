Career

Adames is a well-known professional baseball player. He developed an interest in baseball at a young age. In 2012 Willy was drafted into the Detroit Tigers as an international free agent and made his professional debut with the Dominican Summer League Tigers the following year.

He began the 2014 season with West Michigan but was later assigned to the Bowling Green Hot Rods, where he eventually completed the 2014 season. In 2015, he played with the Charlotte Stone Crabs, where he batted .258 with four home runs and 46 RBIs. The following year he played with Montgomery Biscuits and posted a .274 batting average with 11 home runs, 57 RBIs and 31 doubles.
