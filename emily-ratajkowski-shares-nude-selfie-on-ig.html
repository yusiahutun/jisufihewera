Emily Ratajkowski Bares Her Baby Bump in a Nude Mirror Selfie on Instagram

Emily Ratajkowski is pregnant and proud of it!

The newly announced mother-to-be shared a new nude selfie on Instagram on Tuesday, following her sharing the news that she is expecting her first child with her husband, actor and producer Sebastian Bear-McClard. In the image, the model and actress snaps a sunlit photo of her reflection in a full length mirror, wearing nothing but socks and a pair of gold hoop earrings as she strikes a demure pose.

"20 weeks," Ratajkowski captioned the photo. "getting to know my new body."

Emily Ratajkowski //Instagram

Ratajkowski also showed off her new, sexy maternity style on Monday, opting for a sleek, cut out maxi dress courtesy of Aya Muse, crimson red leather boots by Stuart Weitzman, and chunky gold jewelry for an afternoon out and about in Soho.

Gotham//Getty Images

The model broke the news of her pregnancy yesterday afternoon by sharing a teaser clip of a short film (directed by friend and Girls creator Lena Dunham) narrating her thoughts and emotions amid her first pregnancy. Ratajkowski titled the vignette, Who Will You Be?

"I dreamed of you for the first time the other night," she says in the video. "We are wondering who you will be."

Ratajkowski went on to thank Dunham for helping her bring her baby announcement to life.

"Thank you to my brilliant and very generous friend @lenadunham for her vision and commitment to directing this. I’m so grateful," wrote Ratajkowski. "And thank you to my other wonderful friend @eccopn for his music and to @dschneids for her editing! You all made this the most special. Written and filmed by me. Shot at home."
