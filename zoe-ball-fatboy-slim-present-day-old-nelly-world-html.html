Zoe Ball and her husband Norman Cook described themselves as the happiest couple in the world yesterday as they unveiled their three-day-old daughter Nelly May Lois.

But any toasts to the baby's future are likely to be in nothing stronger than lemonade.

Radio 2 host Miss Ball, 39, and dance music DJ Cook, 46, both gave up alcohol after she spent years trying unsuccessfully for a second child.

Here she is! Norman Cook, aka Fatboy Slim, and wife Zoe Ball present Nelly May Lois Cook to assembled photographers outside the hospital where she gave birth

Hello gorgeous: Proud mum Zoe peels back the layers to reveal more of Nelly's face

The result was Nelly, who arrived by caesarean section on Thursday at a London hospital.

The couple already have a nine-year-old son, Woody.

A spokesman said: 'Mum, Dad and big brother Woody are doing great.'

The couple have had a rocky marriage, to say the least, after reported infidelities on both sides. Cook, better known as Fatboy Slim, has also spent time in rehab for his drink problem.

Miss Ball announced her pregnancy on air in July.

Back in 2000, Zoe and Norman Cook leave the Portland Hospital with their five-day-old son Woody, wearing a Brighton football club hat

She said: 'It's been a long time coming and we're so over the moon. We're feeling blessed because we both gave up drinking and we're thinking this is our gift of sobriety.

'I think we'll both give up for good.'

Miss Ball is to take a two-month break from radio, with former TV host Richard Madeley filling in on her 6am Saturday morning show.
