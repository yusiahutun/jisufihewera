Taylor Swift Wore a Diamond 87 Necklace to the Super Bowl

In case you missed it, Taylor Swift is at the Super Bowl — and she didn't just bring along her posse, she brought a not-so-subtle, very sparkly tribute to her tight end beau, Travis Kelce. While her red jacket had the number 60 (a nod to the Kansas City Chiefs being established in 1960), her blingy diamond necklace was very much a tribute to her boyfriend. Swift arrived at the game in an all-black fit (minus the Chiefs red jacket) and accessorized it with a diamond and yellow gold "87" pendant necklace by Stephanie Gottlieb as well as a diamond tennis choker in rose gold from the brand.

Kelce wears number 87, which would explain the number hanging from Swift's necklace. Like other WAGs, she's not above giving her man a little love. On the other side of today's rivalry, Olivia Culpo wore a coat with her fiancé Christian McCaffrey's number 60 on the back.

Swift's friends are also falling in formation for the Super Bowl. Blake Lively, one of the singer's closest friends, wore a red tracksuit to the big game, another homage to the Chiefs' signature color. Swift and Lively had a few other of their pals join them in their suite, which was reportedly a perk paid for by Kelce to the tune of $2 million. Ice Spice, Lana del Rey, and Brittany Mahomes took in the show from the VIP level alongside Swift and Lively.
