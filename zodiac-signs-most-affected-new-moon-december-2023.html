4 Zodiac Signs Most Affected

The lunar cycle is a journey that constantly ebbs and flows, but its story always begins with the new moon. And if you’re one of the zodiac signs most affected by the new moon, you’re on the verge of making an important decision that has major ramifications for your future. However, if you know what type of energy you’re working with, it’ll be easier to put your best foot forward and maximize your success.

The most dramatic moments of the lunar cycle to happen at two different phases: the new moon and the full moon. While the full moon is always intense and climactic, the new moon is a more gentle nuanced experience, but that doesn’t make it any less important. In fact, the new moon is what kickstarts a whole chapter of your life, as it represents the start of something new. For example: if you start a new relationship or begin the process of committing to a new project during a new moon, there’s a good chance it will reach completion by the time the corresponding full moon takes place six months later. All the emotions and turning points that tend to occur during a new moon are often incredibly telling, revealing all that is in store for you next.

When a new moon takes place, keep in mind that beginnings can feel just as terrifying as endings often feel complicated and bittersweet. If you’re currently feeling intimidated by what the future holds, ground yourself in the present moment and take things one step at a time.

Taking place on December 12 at 6:32 p.m. ET, the upcoming new moon takes place in open-minded, adventurous, truth-seeking, and philosophical Sagittarius, encouraging you to rethink your perspective and the beliefs that guide you forward through life. Because Sagittarius is ruled by Jupiter—planet of growth and grandiose thinking—this new moon may feel like a big splash in your life. And because Jupiter is currently moving through Taurus—a zodiac sign that’s concerned with money and stability—it’s also a great time to set meaningful financial goals and to concentrate your energy on attracting abundance.

Although Sagittarius is generally a positive and optimistic fire sign, this new moon may bring up intense emotions and feelings of uncertainty. After all, this new moon is also squaring off with Neptune—planet of fantasies and distortion—which could leave you feeling fearful and sensitive to the idea of the unknown. And because Mercury is just one day away from stationing retrograde, there’s a good chance you’ll have to take a few steps backward before you can truly move forward. Once again, you’ll have to take things one day at a time.
