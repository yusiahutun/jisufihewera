Is Sea of Thieves cross platform? How to play on different devices

There are numerous cross-platforms gaining popularity worldwide since game developers have focussed more on developing such games. Sea of Thieves (SoT) is one of the games loved by many gamers. But is Sea of Thieves cross platform?

Is Sea of Thieves cross platform on PS4 and Xbox? Which other platform supports this multiplayer game? These are some of the numerous questions gamers wish to have answers to. Here is everything you should know about the fantastic game.

What is Sea of Thieves?

This is a first-person multiplayer game of high-seas piracy and derring-do. It was developed by Rare and published by Microsoft. The game is available on various platforms, including PC, Xbox Series X/S, and Xbox One.

This live-service pirate simulator is one of the best co-op games available today. It was originally an Xbox One game released in 2018. However, it is now also available on PC. The majority of the players you'll run into in-game are console players.

Sea of Thieves is available for free for all players with Xbox Game Pass and across Xbox Series X|S, Xbox One, Windows 10, and Steam.

Is Sea of Thieves cross platform?

This game supports cross platform play across PC and Xbox One. Therefore, with platforms such as Windows 10 and Steam versions on PC, you can crossplay. Other platforms that support it include Xbox One and Xbox Series X versions.

You can play this game with anyone who is playing it from any platform. It is also helpful for new gamers on Xbox or PC because they can easily find their friends and family members despite having different platforms.

Additionally, if you are using an open lobby where random players can join, they can join regardless of the platform they are on.

Is Sea of Thieves cross-platform with PC? You can instantly enable Sea of Thieves crossplay with the simple get-go method. Simply add your friends via the in-game menu to join the lobby and get started.

The procedure for adding your friends on your PC is as follows;

  • Open the Xbox app on Windows
  • Locate the Social tab. It is at the top-left of your screen.
  • Click on the Search for people box.
  • Type your friend's name.
  • Click on their name, and their profile will appear.
  • Click on Add Friend at the far right of the screen.

    • On the other hand, the procedure for adding your friends on your Xbox is as follows;

  • Press the Xbox button on your controller to open the menu.
  • Select the second icon of two people at the top of the menu by scrolling.
  • Click on the Find someone option to search for your friends.

    • After this, you should be able to see each other as added friends. Then you can play the game together.

    Is Sea of Thieves cross-platform on PS4 and PC?

    Sea of Thieves is available to play on PC and not PS4. Therefore, you can use your computer if you do not want to buy an Xbox. But can Xbox One and PC play Sea of Thieves together?

    Cross platform play is supported across numerous platforms. Therefore, playing with your friends across Xbox platforms and PC is possible.

    Is Sea of Thieves cross platform with Nintendo Switch?

    Unfortunately, this multiplayer game is not on Nintendo Switch. Therefore, you can not crossplay on this platform.

    Can Sea of Thieves run on 2 GB RAM?

    This multiplayer game of high-seas piracy can not run on a 2 GB RAM. The minimum system requirements are as follows:

    • Operating System: Windows 10
    • CPU: Intel Iris Pro Graphics 6200 or Intel Iris 540
    • RAM: 4GB
    • GPU: Integrated
    • HDD: 60GB 5.4k RPM
