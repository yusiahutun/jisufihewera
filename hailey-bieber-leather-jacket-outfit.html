Hailey Bieber Wears Leather Jacket &amp; Bucket Hat in West Hollywood

No one does quarantine chic quite like Hailey Bieber.

The model was photographed out and about in West Hollywood on Friday, all bundled up (for Los Angeles's cooler temperatures last week) in a sleek, monochromatic look. Sporting an oversized leather motorcycle jacket by Raey and wide-leg velvet trousers, she finished her ensemble with bright white boots by Doc Marten and a vinyl leather bucket hat by Fiorucci. Bieber also wore a protective mask, as rising cases of COVID-19 continue to greatly affect the state.

BACKGRID

Bieber also took to Instagram to commemorate the all-black look, posting two Polaroid-styled images to the social media app with just her initials, "HRB," in the caption. For those pics, Bieber accessorized the outfit with '90s-esque rectangular-framed sunglasses.

Over the weekend, Bieber shared another monochromatic moment on Instagram, this time opting for an all-white look that included a ribbed knit tank top, an oversized white button-down blouse, and heart-printed tube socks paired with black quilted Chanel platform loafers. Wearing her trusty bucket hat again that day, she accessorized with her signature layered gold chain necklaces, a matching watch, and chunky gold rings.

Lately, Bieber has managed to perfect a few lower-key ensembles as well. During a recent trip to the Bahamas with her husband, musician Justin Bieber, the model wore the perfect pair of hot-pink sweatpants with a matching bikini top, another ribbed tank top, and her favorite white Nike Air Force 1 sneakers.
