2019 Nashville Comedy Festival Brings Jay Leno, Jeff Foxworthy, Sebastian Maniscalco, Ali Wong, Jim

NASHVILLE (CelebrityAccess) – The 2019 Nashville Comedy Festival has today (Nov. 27) announced that it will return to music city the week of April 8 through 14, 2019.

This year’s line-up includes Jay Leno and Jeff Foxworthy, who are set to hit the historic stage of The Opry House on April 14; Sebastian Maniscalco, who is scheduled to perform 2 shows at TPAC’s Polk Theater on April 13; Ali Wong, who makes her Ryman Auditorium debut on April 10; Jim Jefferies, who makes his return to the Ryman Auditorium on April 12; YouTube comedy duo Rhett & Link, who will bring their hit live show to the Ryman Auditorium on April 9; and Tom Segura who takes over Ryman Auditorium for 2 shows on April 14 to close out the festival.

The week-long event will also see the Ryman debut of John Crist for 2 shows, the return of festival favorite Doug Benson, homegrown star Nate Bargatze, the acerbic wit of Janeane Garofalo, comedian/author/actress Rita Rudner, Tiny Meat Gang podcasters Cody Ko & Noel Miller, and rising podcast stars 85 South.

Tickets on sale now at www.nashcomedyfest.com.
