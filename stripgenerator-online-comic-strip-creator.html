StripGenerator: Online Comic Strip Creator

StripGenerator is an online comic strip creator that lets you generate your very own unique comic strips. The site provides you with an original set of characters, and easy to use drag & drop comic strip editor.

Creating strips could not be made easier, all you have to do is grab the items you want to use from the menu and drop on to the frame. You can add unique human characters, beings, objects, shapes and speech bubbles. Scale them, rotate, add colors, add/remove frames and more. Once your comic strip is ready you can print it out or share it with others by publishing it online.

The site doesn't require users to regsiter, however registered users get their own online gallery, some extra strip creating options and more characters to choose from.

You can also browse and view strips created by other users, rate them and leave comments.

Features:

  • Free comic strip creator.
  • Create as many strips as you like.
  • Intuitive strip creator with drag & drop interface.
  • Add unique human characters, beings, objects, shapes and speech bubbles with text.
  • Print your strips or publish online.
  • Share your strip by forwarding a link or embedding.
  • No registration necessary, however if registered you will get extra features.
  • Browse strips of other users rate and leave comments.
  • Similar sites: Dilbert, Pixton, Comiqs and MakeBeliefsComix.

Check out StripGenerator @ www.stripgenerator.com
