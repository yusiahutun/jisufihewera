It has been said that you can never quite forget about your first love, but it seems as if P. Diddy might have.

TMZ is reporting that the mother of the 44-year-old entrepreneur's first child is having her home in Westchester, New York foreclosed in on by the bank.

Misa Hylton-Brim is the mother of rappers's firstborn son, Justin Combs.

Tough news: TMZ is reporting that, Misa Hylton-Brim,  the mother of Diddy's son Justin is having her home in Westchester, New York foreclosed in on by the bank

Cash cow: The rapper topped the Forbes list of highest paid hip-hop artists last year by making $50 million

According to documents obtained by the entertainment site, Diddy - born Sean Combs - bought the posh 3-bedroom, 3,215 square feet house for his ex.

However, the bank stopped receiving payments for the property in 2010.

Legal documents also say that Diddy took out a $712,000 mortgage and owed $622,518 as of 2010, which has since been amassing interest since.

TMZ is also reporting that the bank wants all of the money or the house will be going up for auction.

It seems like it isn't the money holding payments back as Diddy topped the Forbes list of highest paid hip-hop artists in September by pulling in $50 million in the twelve months previous.

Up to this point, it has seemed that the Last Night singer and the mother of his son have had a cordial relationship.

In September of 2012, Diddy even shared a snap on Instagram of the pair together at their son's American football game.

Diddy puts the bunny ears behind his baby mama's head as they were both proudly draped in gear from the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA).

The 20-year-old son of Sean and Misa is currently on a scholarship at the university.

The rapper captioned the image: 'Me and my first girlfriend and the mother to my child. Friends forever.'
