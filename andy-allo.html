About

Andy Allo is a 35 year old American Actress. Born on 13th January, 1989 in Bamenda, Northwest Region, Cameroon and educated at Arden Middle School and El Camino Fundamental High School, she is famous for her contribution to Prince's backing band New Power Generation, her solo albums UnFresh and Superconductor, and her roles as Serenity in the 2017 musical comedy film Pitch Perfect 3, Lieutenant Wendy Seager in the NBC action drama series Chicago Fire, and Nora Antony in the Amazon Prime Video science fiction comedy series Upload. Andy Allo (born January 13, 1989) is a Cameroonian-American singer-songwriter, guitarist and actress. She released her first of three albums in 2009, and joined Prince's band, The New Power Generation, in 2011. She had a recurring role in three episodes of the comedy-drama series The Game in 2011, followed by a number of other roles, including a supporting role in the 2017 film Pitch Perfect 3 and the lead role of Nora in Amazon Prime's series Upload (2020).
