Natacha Rambova and Alvaro De Urzaiz

Alvaro De Urzaiz is a Spanish Personality.

American Dancer Natacha Rambova was born Winifred Kimball Shaughnessy Hudnut on 19th January, 1897 in Salt Lake City, Utah and passed away on 5th Jun 1966 Pasadena, California aged 69. She is most remembered for wife of Rudolph Valentino. Her zodiac sign is Capricorn.

(1934 - 1939) (divorced)
Rambova met Alvaro de Urzaiz on a trip to Europe in 1934. Urzaiz was a British educated, Spanish aristocrat who physically resembled Valentino. After closing her shop, Rambova moved with her husband to the island of Mallorca. When the Spanish Civil War erupted, Urzaiz was on the pro-fascists nationalist side, becoming a naval commander. Rambova initially shared his views, but changed after she witnessed a pro Franco bishop who wouldn’t provide sanctuary to a woman when leftists were rounding up people and shooting. Rambova took pictures of the destruction she witnessed during this time.
Rambova fled to Nice, where she suffered a heart attack at age 40. Soon after her and Urzaiz divorced, another childless marriage.
