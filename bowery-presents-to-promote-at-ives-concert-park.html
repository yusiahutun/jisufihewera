Bowery Presents To Promote At Ives Concert Park

DANBURY, CT (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — The board of directors at the Ives Concert Park have signed an exclusive promotion deal with New York-based promoter Bowery Presents.

First opened in 1975, the 6,000 capacity outdoor concert venue is located on the Western Connecticut State University Westside campus in Danbury and hosts live events throghout the year, including a summer concert series.

The Bowery Presents operates a number of venues in the Pacific northwest and the New York metropolitan region, including Terminal 5, The Bowery Ballroom, Mercury Lounge, Music Hall of Williamsburg and the Wellmont Theatre in Montclair, N.J.

Jim Glancy, partner at The Bowery Presents, said, “The Bowery Presents is excited to promote concerts in Western Connecticut at a beautiful outdoor venue such as the Ives. We look forward to announcing the lineup for summer 2013 in the near future.” – CelebrityAccess Staff Writers
