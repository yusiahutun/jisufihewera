Mexican singer Patricia Navidad whose sanitary towel fell out on live TV is a 'hero'

A Mexican singer has shown the perfect way to talk down bullies after she was trolled on social media when a sanitary towel fell between her legs during a TV appearance. 

Patricia Navidad, 42, had been wearing a short blue mini dress while appearing on the morning TV show called Despierta America (Wake Up America). 

After the incident was broadcast, several men got in contact on her Twitter page to mock her. 

Mexican singer Patricia Navidad's sanitary pad began to fall from between her legs during a live TV performance 

But Patricia took the incident in her stride with a series of clever put-downs that left her critics looking ridiculous and saw her hailed as a 'queen.' 

In a dig at their lack of manners, the star responded: 'They say that a man who treats a woman like a princess was raised by a queen, those who attack, who raised you?' 

After rightly stating that the accident was nothing to be embarrassed of, she went on to say the men hiding behind their keyboards wouldn't be so outspoken f they were looking at her face to face.
