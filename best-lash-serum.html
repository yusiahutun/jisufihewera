A Lash &amp; Brow Serum That Won't Irritate Eyes

Contact lenses and NYC air pollution certainly don't help, but I've always had extra-sensitive, redness-prone eyes—it's not unusual for people to ask me if I've been crying after I've only just rubbed my lid or come inside from a windy day. A few months ago, when I told an aesthetician at The Organic Pharmacy that I wake up to puffy, irritated eyes no matter what eye cream I use, she suggested I take a break from all creams at night and avoid the eye area completely. As someone who lives by a "more is more" mantra when it comes to skin care, it was hard—but it worked. Now, I'm seeing if I can slowly build up to using night repair products again (one key is to apply an hour before hitting the pillow to allow your skin time to absorb them).

What's working? This lash and brow conditioning serum—one of the best ones I've tried. I love the lipgloss-like applicator, which distributes the formula more easily and thoroughly than the tiny brushes on others and never gets in my eyes, as much as my newly strengthened lashes. They aren't longer (yet—I've been using it for three weeks), but they're thicker and have stopped falling out when I remove my mascara. Best of all, absolutely no irritation.

Shiseido Full Lash Serum, $35, shiseido.com.
