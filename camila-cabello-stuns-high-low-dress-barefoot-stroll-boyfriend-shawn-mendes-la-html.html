Camila Cabello stuns in a high-low dress on a barefoot stroll with her boyfriend Shawn Mendes in LA

While she was away, the Canadian heart-throb revealed on the The Kyle and Jackie O Show, he was house sitting for his girlfriend at her pad in the Hollywood Hills. 

'I haven't seen her for a month and a half, so I'm pretty excited,' he told the hosts, one day ahead of Cabello's return to the United States in early October.

Shawn and Camila were friends for several years before they became romantically linked in July last year, after collaborating on their massive hit, Señorita.

Camila was previously in a long-term relationship with dating guru, Matthew Hussey, before they split in 2019.

The Fifth Harmony alum's former bandmate, Ally Brooke, recently revealed in her new book that their hit girl group almost replaced Cabello, after she left to embark on a solo career, and spoke about it in an interview with StyleCaster. 

Finding Your Harmony: Ally Brooke, 27, reveals in her new book that Fifth Harmony had the option to replace Camila Cabello, 23, when she left the to pursue a solo career in 2016

In Brooke's memoir, Finding Your Harmony, she opened up about that pivotal time in the group's history, explaining there were 'several different options and routes' they could have used to replace her, including a reality search competition. 

In the end she and remaining members Normani Kordei, 24, Dinah Jane, 23, and Lauren Jauregui, 24, decided to push forward as a foursome. 

Since her departure in 2016, Cabello's solo career has taken off, with hit songs Havana and Senorita launching her to the top of the charts.
