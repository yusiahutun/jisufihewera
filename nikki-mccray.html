Who is Nikki McCray:

Nikki McCray is a famous Basketball Player. She was born on December 17, 1971 and her birthplace is Collierville, TN. Nikki is also well known as, Former professional women’s basketball player who made three WNBA All-Star teams as a member of teams like the Washington Mystics and Phoenix Mercury. She also won gold medals representing the United States at the 1996 and 2000 Olympic Games.
