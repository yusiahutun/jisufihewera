Emily Ratajkowski Is So Chic in a Black Miniskirt and Over-the-Knee Boots

Emily Ratajkowski stepped out for a Halloween stroll looking more chic than spooky.

The model was seen walking around New York City with baby son Sylvester Apollo Bear. For the outing, she chose an all-black look made up of a turtleneck top with mid-length sleeves, a black denim miniskirt, and leather thigh-high boots with chunky heels. She accessorized simply with a leather belt with a gold buckle, a mini black cross-body bag, a gold bracelet, and gold hoop earrings. For glam, she opted for natural rosy makeup, dark eyes, and a red lip, and she wore her straight hair down.

Contrasting her dark, sophisticated look was baby Sly's happy, vibrant fit. The one-year-old looked adorable in a white sweater with red hearts, white sweatpants with black hearts, little black boots, and a red cape.

Splash News

Ratajkowski shared two TikTok videos of her in the ensemble, including one with the sound of Taylor Swift's chart-topping hit "Karma." She wrote in the post caption, "KARMA IS MY BOYFRIEND."

In the other, she was also seen singing and dancing while showing off the look.

The model recently split from her husband of four years, Sebastian Bear-McClard, following reports that he was unfaithful. And in a recent interview for Harper's BAZAAR's November cover, she revealed that this is her first time being truly single and that she has "gone on dates."

Ratajkowski's outing with her son came hours after she celebrated Halloween by partying with friends in a sultry cowgirl outfit. The model wore a black crop top paired with a pair of fringed chaps, showing off a pair of matching underwear underneath. She completed the look with a black cowboy hat. She shared a TikTok video of her strutting through the streets of Manhattan in the costume, accompanied by best friend Babs, who dressed as rapper Bad Bunny in his sunshine look from his "Neverita" music video.
