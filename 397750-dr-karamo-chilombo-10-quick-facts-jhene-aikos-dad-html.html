Dr. Karamo Chilombo: 10 quick facts about Jhene Aiko's dad

Dr. Karamo Chilombo, otherwise known as Dr. Chill, is a trained American medical specialist and singer. He is popularly known as the father of singers Jhene Aiko and Mila J. His love for what he does is among the main reasons why many of his fans would love to know more about him.

Doctor Chilombo is, undeniably, not your ordinary doctor. His inspirational posts on social media and music will leave you baffled. Also, he has a unique dressing code. For instance, his immense love for Rasta colour and blue attires would fool you to believing he is a Jamaican national.

Chilombo is a diligent fellow with an incredible life story. Many people, probably, do not know much about him, especially when he is off the screens and social media. Here are the top 10 facts from Dr. Karamo Chilombo biography you might find interesting:

Many people have known him as Karamo Chilombo. Others know him as Dr. Chill O.G, which is his stage name. These are not his birth names. Jhene Aiko's dad changed his identity at the age of 20.

What does the name Chilombo mean? One may ask. It is a name with an interesting meaning. Early 2020, her daughter released an album titled Chilombo. While explaining to Billboard why she titled the album using her dad’s name, Jhene said,

3. He is of mixed ethnicity

Where is Dr Karamo Chilombo from? He is from Bakersfield, California, U.S.A. He is of mixed ethnicity – his father is of African-American, German Jewish descent while his mother is of Spanish, Japanese, and Dominican descent.

Usually, he likes to talk about African culture on his social media pages. He recently released a song titled The Sounds of Mother Africa. Similarly, he was among the public figures who took part in the Black Lives Matter campaign after the death of George Perry Floyd Jr.

First, he graduated from the University Of California San Francisco School Of Medicine in 1974. Later in 1978, he also graduated from Case Western Reserve University School Of Medicine, Cleveland, Ohio.

5. He is a practising medical doctor

Chilombo is a licenced medical specialist. He obtained his certifications and licensure in 1978. Currently, he is working at Los Angeles Community Hospital at LA. He is also affiliated to Monterey Park Hospital.

Is Dr. Karamo Chilombo a paediatrician? Yes, he is in the paediatrics department; a field that assesses and treats infants, children, and adolescents. He has been practising for over four decades now.

6. He is a proud father of six

The 76-year-old is a dad to six children- four daughters and two sons. The eldest is around 39 years old while the youngest is 32 years old.

The names of Dr. Karamo Chilombo children are Miyoko (born in 1981), Mila J (born on 1983), Jahi (born in 1984), Miyagi (born in 1986), Kareena, and Jhene Aiko (born in 1988). Sadly, Miyagi Chilombo succumbed to cancer in 2012.
