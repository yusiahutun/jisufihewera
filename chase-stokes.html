Educational Qualifications

Chase Stokes has done his schooling at Timber Creek High School, Orlando, Florida. Then he went to Valencia College in Florida followed by the University of Central Florida.

Family & Relationships

FAMILY:

Chase Stokes’s father’s name is ‘Jeff Stokes’ and his mother’s name is ‘Jennifer Canning’. His parents got divorced when Chase was very young. Chase moved with his mother to Atlanta, Georgia, and later to Orlando.

Chase’s stepmother is ‘Nicki Osterman Stokes’ and he has a younger brother, Kendall Nicole, and a younger brother, Kaden. He has three sisters named ‘Rylie Walker’, ‘Griffin Walker’ and ‘Evan Walker’.

RELATIONSHIPS:

It was known that Chase Stokes was dating Xio Montalvo but they split in the years 2017.
