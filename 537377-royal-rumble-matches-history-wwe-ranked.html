20 best Royal Rumble matches in the history of WWE, ranked

Since its inception in 1988, the Royal Rumble match has become one of the most anticipated and thrilling events in professional wrestling. The competition features the best competitors vying for a championship opportunity at WrestleMania. But have you ever wondered which have been the best Royal Rumble matches in the history of the WWE?

The Royal Rumble match is a one-of-a-kind event that has become a staple of WWE's annual pay-per-view lineup. The match begins with two wrestlers in the ring, and every 90 seconds, a new superstar enters the fray.

The goal is to eliminate opponents by tossing them over the top rope onto the arena floor. The last man standing in the ring is declared the winner and earns a shot at the WWE Championship at WrestleMania.

Best Royal Rumble matches

Royal Rumble has provided fans with countless unforgettable matches, from the first-ever Royal Rumble in 1988 to the most recent edition. What is the best Royal Rumble in history? Here are 20 of the best matches of all time.

Considered by many to be the greatest Rumble match ever, this event featured an incredible lineup of superstars. The match is also considered the most star-studded Royal Rumble ever. It featured top athletes, including Ric Flair, The Undertaker, Randy Savage, and many other top wrestlers.
