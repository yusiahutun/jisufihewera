About

Natalia Lafourcade is a 40 year old Mexican Singer. Born María Natalia Lafourcade Silva on 26th February, 1984 in Mexico City, Distrito Federal, Mexico, she is famous for her original pop-rock compositions and traditional folk music interpretations, in addition to her hits such as her Latin Grammy award-winning hit song Hasta la Raiz, which is also the name of her 2015 album, and other various songs, including En el 2000, Un derecho de nacimiento, Nunca es sufficient, Tú sí sabe quererme, De todas las flores, Elefantes, and Busca un Problema, as well as her performance of the Oscar-winning song Remember Me, from the 2017 3D computer-animated fantasy comedy-drama film Coco in a career that spans 1998–present.
