100 Good Morning Quotes to Give Your Day a Bright Start

Getting out of the bed in the morning can be a challenge, to say the least, so to help remedy that, we've rounded up 100 good morning quotes to help start your day off on a bright note.

Each of these inspirational quotes about life will give you a unique reason to smile at the possibility of the day that lies ahead and all the sunshine it could hold, infusing the start of your routines with optimism. And if it's not just any morning but a Monday morning, these Monday quotes will help them feel a little less Monday-ish, and so will these happy quotes too. Drawing from the words of poets like Ralph Waldo Emerson and Emily Dickinson, plus notable figures like Mahatma Gandhi and Nelson Mandela, these positive sayings will set you up for success in your day and bring a dose of awesome to even the most mundane of agendas. Gratitude, as articulated in these quotes, goes a long way to lighten the load of any day from the start too. Even if you will never never love the first hours of the day (not everyone can be a morning person!), they are worth checking out.

And now for those good morning quotes and the sunshine they bring!
