Dierks Bentley Launches Clothing Line Called Desert Son

Dierks Bentley is releasing a new album and going on tour soon, and he recently opened his own Nashville bar. Still, he figured, why not a clothing line?

Desert Son is the star's new lifestyle collection with Flag & Anthem, announced this week. The line — inspired by some of the lyrics from his The Mountain album — will launch later this month, and it'll be versatile, Bentley promises. The collection boasts mostly men's items including vintage-washed tees, raglans, hoodies and hats. The "Woman, Amen" singer hasn't completely forgotten the ladies, however — women's T-shirts are also available.

Bentley promises a summer and fall collection as well, which will include button-down shirts, knit tops, denim and jackets. Bentley and his longtime stylist, Annie Psaltiras, helped to create the line which aligns with Bentley's personal dress style.

"This has been such a fun thing to create and develop," he says. "I love clothes that are perfectly worn-in like you’ve had them a lifetime, but can also take the wear and tear that comes from an active lifestyle. I’ve learned over the last few months that there’s a real art to getting that just right. The Flag & Anthem guys have been doing it at the highest level and have helped me put together a collection that I think is totally authentic to me and one that hopefully people all over the country will dig too."

Desert Son is available at Buckle, Von Maur and Dillard’s and online.
