Why The Smell of Fish in Your House Is a Health Risk

Feb 18, 2024 Fish is a notoriously smelly food. But unless you’re cooking it every day, a fishy odor that lingers for days, weeks, or months could be a sign that something isn’t quite right with your electrics — and it shouldn’t be dismissed. It might sound odd, but a fishy smell can be an early indicator of a serious electrical problem that could potentially lead to a fire. When a circuit develops a fault and overheats, the plastics and heat-resistant chemicals used in sockets, circuit breakers, or wiring insulation often emit a fishy or urine-like smell.
