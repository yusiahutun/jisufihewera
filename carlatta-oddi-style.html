A Boho Babe Wears Her Go-To Cardigan, Four Chic Ways

Carlotta Oddi found fashion fame the good old-fashioned way: by being a stylist assistant that was so-well dressed, the street style paps made her a star in her own right. Oddi may have worked under Anna Dello Russo at Vogue Japan, but the style she has become synonymous with couldn’t be more different—while her magpie boss is well-known for her penchant for glitzy, straight-from-the-runway, head-to-toe looks, Oddi is a true bohemian at heart. When asked to describe her aesthetic, the Milanese-born Oddi says she doesn't like to stick to a precise dress code. “I guess chameleonic/eclectic," she explains. "I really like to mix and match different pieces, to combine a romantic dress with punk studded shoes, a Chanel bag with some tribal vintage jewelry. I love to discover small brands and love finding the perfect piece in a vintage store or flea market. In general, I really like crossover in life—of different worlds and inspirations.”

That style served as the inspiration behind Alanui, a stylish sibling endeavor. Her brother Nicolò Oddi developed the knitwear brand, which centers around the cardigan, with Carlotta, who acts as muse and number one fan. It stands to reason then, that an Alanui cardigan—which translates to "large path" in Hawaiian—is the current most-loved piece in Carlotta’s enviable closet. “I just love it! It's one of the first cardigans we made when we started Alanui. The dark colors mixed with pale yellow basically go with any occasion. From a day look to evening attire… my brother Nicolò even used it as a tuxedo jacket for a gala. It really goes with everything...” See how Oddi wears her wardrobe MVP four ways.

Look 1: Evening Cocktail Look

Courtesy of Carlotta Oddi

Alanui cardigan, Attico dress, Gucci shoes

Why did you want to launch Alanui? What was the inspiration behind it?

"Travel is the inspiration behind Alanui. The entire project started from a trip Nicolò made to California, where he found this vintage cardigan that he ended up giving me for a trip I was planning to Hawaii. I wore it the entire time. I just could not take it off. The word ALANUI means “large path” in Hawaiian and we are aiming to create an item that is the perfect travel companion, a life-time piece that can follow you in all your experiences and journeys. It’s kind of a 'Linus blanket' that grows and connects with you, even emotionally."

Look 2: Easy and Basic Day Look

Courtesy of Carlotta Oddi

Zoe Karssen t-shirt, Frame Denim jeans, Chanel shoes, Venus by Maria Tash earrings, Aliita ring, Jessie Western bracelets

Why is knitwear an important category for you?
"Knitwear is extremely versatile, you can play with it and style it in different ways, but it remains a comfortable, beautiful item."

Look 3: Perfect Day-to-Night Look

Courtesy of Carlotta Oddi

Alanui cardigan, Saint Laurent dress, Saint Laurent belt, Frame denim jeans, Chanel shoes, Jessie Western necklaces

Do you plan your outfits in advance? What does getting dressed in the morning look like?
"I am not the kind of girl who prepares and decides her outfit the night before. Usually I am always on the run, so I pick the easiest/simplest clothes for the day. It’s different when I travel, especially if I travel for work or fashion week. I usually plan my outfits in advance and try to make combinations to use pieces more than once. I do prefer to travel light and not to bring things that I am not wearing. And you always need to save some space in case you find the perfect dress you were looking for months!"

Full of course, but perfectly organized!
