Best White Sneakers for Women 2023

Every product on this page was chosen by a Harper's BAZAAR editor. We may earn commission on some of the items you choose to buy.

Valentino Open for a Change Low-Rise Sneaker

Valentino Open for a Change Low-Rise Sneaker

Double the impact on your wardrobe and consider investing in a pair of white sneakers with a cause. Twenty percent of the proceeds from Valentino Garavani's Open for a Change low-rise style goes to re:wild, an organization dedicated to restoring the planet.

Tod's Slip-On Sneaker

Tod's Slip-On Sneaker

Credit: Tod's

A lightweight alternative to classic leather styles, Tod's slip-on sneakers are made from a knit fabric that's well suited for warmer weather. Translation: You'll be able to wear them throughout the summer months.

Atoms Model 000 Sneaker

Atoms Model 000 Sneaker

Reimagining the traditional sizing chart, Atoms is committed to customers finding their perfect fit. The brand uses advanced technology to ensure its sneakers are as comfortable as they are aesthetically pleasing.

Reebok Classic Leather 1983 Vintage Shoe

Reebok Classic Leather 1983 Vintage Shoe

Looking for a retro-inspired pair? Reebok's reissue of its 1983 sneakers has all the makings of your new everyday signature. The slim silhouette looks just as great with jeans as it does a pleated midi skirt.

The Row Marie H Leather Sneaker

The Row Marie H Leather Sneaker

Leave it to The Row to design an ultra-chic sneaker that is more polished than sporty. The thick platformed sole will keep longline trousers from dragging when you walk.

Nike Air Force 1 '07

Nike Air Force 1 '07

The Air Force 1 is a tried-and-true classic. A fresh white pair looks great with everything from tailored separates to a sweat suit off duty.

No. 21 Billy Sneaker

No. 21 Billy Sneaker

The Italian brand's Billy sneaker made its debut back in 2018, but it's maintained its spot as a top contender in the dad sneaker category. Try wearing yours with cropped trousers and an oversized shirt.

Acne Studios Bolzter W Tumbled Sneaker

Acne Studios Bolzter W Tumbled Sneaker

For the new season, Acne Studios churned out a pair of tactile sneakers made from leather with a tumbled-effect and gripped sole.

New Balance XC-72 Sneaker

New Balance XC-72 Sneaker

Embrace the New Balance resurgence with a pair of two-tone sneakers set atop a gum sole. Cuffed jeans and a plain white tee are ideal outfit accompaniments.

Converse High-Top Chuck Taylor All Star Sneaker

Converse High-Top Chuck Taylor All Star Sneaker

It doesn't get much more timeless than a pair of Chuck Taylors. The high-top style looks particularly great tucked under cropped hemlines.

Saint Laurent Court Classic Signature Sneaker

Saint Laurent Court Classic Signature Sneaker

Logos not really your thing? Consider going with a pair of classic Saint Laurents instead.

Veja White & Blue V-12 Sneaker

Veja White & Blue V-12 Sneaker
