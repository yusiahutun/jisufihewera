Alexander Skarsgard and Kate Bosworth have called an end to their relationship after nearly two years of dating.

Their decision to split is said to have been 'very mutual' according to Us Magazine.

'It happened a while ago,' a source told the U.S. publication of the famous  couple.

Breakup: Alexander Skarsgard and Kate Bosworth, pictured at the Coachella festival back in April, are reported to have split

Alexander, 34, and Kate, 28, were last pictured together back in April when they both attended the Coachella Music festival in Indio, California.

Now the True Blood star is enjoying the single life and is reported to have flirted with a pretty brunette at a Comic Con party in San Diego this weekend.

'He's single,' confirms a source. 'And he is loving the attention.'

Post split: The Swedish actor, who attended Comic Con this weekend, is now said to be enjoying the single life

Fan favourite: Alexander (second left) poses with the True Blood cast including Ryan Kwanten (far right), and Anna Paquin (second right) at the fan convention in San Diego

Alexander was at Comic Con to attend a panel for the hit HBO show in which he plays vampire Eric Northman.

He looked relaxed as he joined fellow cast members Anna Paquin, Ryan Kwanten and Stephen Moyer at the fan convention.

Meanwhile, 21 actress Kate was earlier this month photographed hugging her ex-boyfriend Orlando Bloom, who is now married to Australian model Miranda Kerr.

However, the former couple will be seen on screen when their delayed thriller movie Straw Dogs is released in September.
