What age difference? Jennifer Aniston, 44, and Emma Roberts, 22, show off long lean legs in clinging mini skirts while promoting new movie

There may be a 22 year age gap between them, but Jennifer Aniston proved she is more than a match for her younger We're The Millers co-star Emma Roberts in the legs department.

The 44-year-old actress showed off her perfectly toned pins in a racy leather mini-skirt as she arrived at a New York press conference to promote the film on Saturday.

Sporting her favourite colour - black- Jennifer looked typically chic in a matching blazer, chunky gold chain necklace and silver and black and gold spiky sandals.

Hell for leather: Jennifer Aniston showed her toned pins in a black mini skirt, while her co-star Emma Roberts sported a metallic pencil skirt as they arrived at a press conference in New York on Saturday

Emma also opted for a largely black ensemble - sporting a black top, heels and tight pale gold leather skirt with a daring split.

With her hair in an intricate high ponytail, the 22-year-old blossming actress smiled and waved to onlookers.

She looked a little more relaxed than her co-star who marched in to the venue, hiding her eyes behind dark sunglasses.

Favourite hue: Jennifer rarely departs from more conservative colours, on Saturday teaming her ensemble with a chunky gold necklace

Slim and trim: Svelte Emma plays a runaway teen girl in the new comedy movie

They were joined at the event by co-star Jason Sudeikis who sported a baseball cap, sunglasses, blazer and jeans as he grabbed a coffee before the press event.

We’re The Millers sees Jennifer and Emma pretend to be mother and daughter in order to help out with a drug shipment.

Jennifer plays a stripper while Emma portrays a runaway, with their attempts at acting like a family quickly going awry.

Coffee run: We're the Millers co-star Jason Sudeikis looked ultra casual in a baseball cap, sunglasses, blazer and jeans as he made his way to the press call

Jennifer's appearance on Saturday came two days after her stunning arrival at the New York premiere where she wore a plum satin Burberry strapless dress.

Although she walked the carpet solo, she was later spotted emerging from the film's afterparty with fiancé Justin Theroux.

The couple have been engaged for a year, and Jennifer recently explained that they are in no rush to tie the knot.

She said: 'We just want to do it when it's perfect, and we're not rushed, and no one is rushing from a job or rushing to a job. We have yet to set any date.'

The movie is due to be released in the US on August 7 and the UK on August 23.
