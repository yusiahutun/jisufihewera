Dirty Harry’s secret is out! Clint Eastwood is dating a woman named Christina Sandera, a source reveals to Us Weekly. The 84-year-old actor-director and hostess were last photographed together shopping at an L.A. Whole Foods on June 1.

“She lives with him in his house he used to share with Dina,” the insider tells Us. “She moved in months ago.” (Sandera works at Eastwood’s Mission Ranch Hotel in Carmel, Calif.)

“Clint’s kids have all met Christina and like [her],” according to the insider. The “kids all say she is ‘normal.'”

This is Eastwood’s second relationship since splitting with TV news anchor wife Dina Eastwood. Last August, Us broke news of the couple’s separation.
