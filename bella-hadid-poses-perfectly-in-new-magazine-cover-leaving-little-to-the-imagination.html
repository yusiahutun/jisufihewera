Bella Hadid poses perfectly in new magazine cover leaving little to the imagination

Bella Hadid is ‘back in action’ and proves once again she’s perfect for any magazine cover – pardon the pun. After taking a break from modeling to focus on her health, and she’s leaving little to the imagination as she graces her first magazine cover since her hiatus.

The 27-year-old, younger sister of Gigi Hadid took a hiatus in her career, whilst she underwent treatment for Lyme disease. The star showed off many iconic and incredible looks for Perfect Magazine, but one caught the eye of fans, where she left ‘little to the imagination’.

Bella Hadid graces Perfect Magazine cover after health hiatus

The star took to her Instagram to post several looks from the shoot, but there was one Perfect Magazine cover that gained everyone’s attention.

Perfect stated the look, in which the model was covering her body with a poster that read “Absolutely Nothing, Nothing At All” was inspired by “an image of Debbie Harry performing in 1978, wearing a dress a poster she had torn from the wall outside the venue.”

Underneath, the model was wearing nothing but underwear, as she left ‘nothing at all’ to the imagination in the shoot which she art-directed herself.

In the stunning snap, Hadid could be seen wearing big gold hoop earrings, as her hair cascaded past her shoulder in effortless waves. She looked stunningly natural in minimal makeup, as her rosy red cheeks were enhanced with blush.

Other pics showed the 27-year-old wearing a white T-shirt which read “Screaming in Perfect English,” and another with herself holding her hair up in a simple, yet effective pink tee.

Being the supportive sister that she is, Gigi, who is rumored to be dating Bradley Cooper, rushed to the comments on Bella’s posts, as she wrote: “Gorgeousness.”

The model shows off her toned bikini-bod

Just days before the timeline was graced with the star’s cover shoot, Bella posted a racy Instagram snap of herself, showing off her toned abs.

She posed in a tiny black bikini, showing off her toned figure, whilst once again sporting the natural look with minimal makeup.

Fans took to the comments to admire the model, as one penned: “Gorgeous, bod amazing, skin healthy, hair gorgeous, arms toned. Literally ate this.”

“So this is pretty much the look I’m going for,” joked another.
