Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce 'shut down gym' to enjoy it for themselves

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce shut down an LA gym to enjoy a two-hour workout session, reports claim.

The music star and NFL tight end have been enjoying the free time they have together now the Super Bowl is over and the singer takes a break with her Eras Tour. Taylor Swift and Travis were seen packing on the PDA while soaking up the sunshine in the Bahamas last week. This was before they headed out for a spot of lunch at Nobu in Malibu. Now the 34-year-old and Travis Kelce are making sure they get their workouts in.

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce ‘shut down LA gym’

The Cruel Summer singer and NFL athlete shut down Dogpound, the gym in West Hollywood, California on Sunday.

Witnesses claimed members were waiting outside for ‘two hours’ until they finishing their training session, however, the establishment has blasted these claims as false.

They say they’ve never made their members wait outside for two hours, so it may be the pair had a booking outside of normal operating hours.

Dogpound said: “At DOGPOUND, we value our  members experience and have never had anyone wait outside for 2 hours.

“The Daily Mail article does not accurately reflect the circumstances that day. We respect the privacy of our clients and have no other comments to share.”

The celebrity couple have been lapping up the rays in LA after their romantic trip, where they stayed at the Rosalita House on Harbour Island in the Bahamas. It’s said to cost $15,000 a night, and has six bedrooms, a gym, a pool and butlers.

Fans fool for fake video of couple ‘falling over’

Before Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce headed to the gym, fans were duped into thinking a video of the pair falling over at the beach were the couple.

A Twitter user posted the clip, of what appears to show a man and a woman enjoying a romantic swing on the sunny beach before it tips over and they fall onto the sand in an embarrassing blip.

The user claimed it was Taylor and Travis, but didn’t tell fans it was an older video of an entirely different couple.

Many Swifties were fooled into thinking it was true – but it’s not their fault, considering the tweet named them.
